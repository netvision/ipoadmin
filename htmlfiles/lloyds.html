Incorporation

Company was originally incorporated as a Private Limited Company under the name “Lloyds Luxuries Private Limited” on October 21, 2013. Subsequently company was converted into a Public Limited Company and consequently the name of the Company was changed to “Lloyds Luxuries Limited.”

History

  • 2013: Execution of franchisee agreement with Truefitt & Hill (Gentlemen’s Grooming) Limited, UK.
  • 2015-16: Won Indian Salon and Wellness Awards for Design for the Beauty Wellness Enterprise by the Wella Professionals.
  • 2017-18: Won Times Retails Icon 2017 for Luxury Men’s Salon for “Truefitt & Hill”.
  • 2019-20:
    • Execution of franchisee agreement with Mary Cohr, France.
    • Crossed Revenue of Rs. 25 crores.

Holding and Subsidiary

  • Plutus Trading & Commodities LLP is Holding Company.
  • Company does not have any Subsidiaries, Associates, or joint ventures.

Business of the Company

  • Company is amongst the organized player of salon services and beauty products in India.
  • It offers salon services such as haircare, skincare, bath and body products, professional hair products and salon styling tools.
  • All major products are manufactured by Master franchisor and imported in India.
  • Retail operations in respect of men grooming products are carried out through its owned stores, sub-franchisees as well as through online channels.
  • It has 27 stores, of which 14 are run by it directly and 13 through sub-franchisee.
  • Company also have internationally reach through opening of first barber store in Dhaka, Bangladesh as sub-franchisee store.

Franchisee

  • Truefitt & Hill
    1. It is an international brand offering a wide range of beauty products and salon services for men.
    2. It operates 14 barber stores under the brand Truefitt & Hill across 7 cities in India.
    3. It offers haircut, hair spa, hairstyling, facial, manicure, pedicure & more such services.
    4. It offers men’s grooming products with over 100 products.
  • MARY COHR
    1. It offers Women’s Grooming services and Products.
    2. It is operating 1 store under the brand name MARY CHOR.
    3. It offers wide range of salon services and products like face care products, body care products, hair removal products etc.

Property Details:

  • Its 15 stores in India (including one of MARY COHR), all are in leased premises with lease contracts of five to ten year.
