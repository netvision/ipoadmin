Incorporation

Company was originally incorporated on March 08, 2021 as “Shashwat Furnishing Solutions Private Limited”. Subsequently, Company has acquired the entire business with the assets and liabilities of M/s Handicrafts Village, sole proprietorship concern of Promoter Mr. Hitesh Karnawat vide Business Transfer Agreement dated June 01, 2021. Thereafter Company was converted into Public Limited Company and consequently name of company was changed to “Shashwat Furnishing Solutions Limited” and a fresh certificate of incorporation dated August 27, 2021 issued by the Registrar of Companies, Jaipur.

Holdings and Subsidiaries:

  • Company does not have any holding, subsidiary and joint venture.

Business of the Company

  • Company is engaged in processing of semi-finished furniture products into finished products and manufacturing through job work of furniture and handicraft items.
  • It is specialized in developing a qualitative range of Industrial Furniture and Restaurant and Cafe Furniture, Garden and Outdoor Furniture Vintage Furniture, Mirror Frames, Wall clocks and various other decoration & handicrafts objects.
  • Company sales its products to wholesalers, retailers and customers through online platforms such as India Mart, Trade India, Exporter India etc.
  • Products of the company are cherished by the corporate sector and widely utilized for gifting and decorative purpose in the hospitality industry

Business Process

  1. Material Handling: Material handling i.e., procurement of semi-finished products from manufacturers.
  2. Assembling/ Gluing: Assembly involves the fitting together of semi-finished parts into a complete unit.
  3. Rubbing and Sanding: Rubbing is the cutting down with sandpaper of the top coat to make it completely level.
  4. Finishing: There are various methods through which finishing is done like brushing, spraying, pressing, dipping, printing etc
  5. Inspection and Packing: A high-quality inspection process ensures the products are checked for against all specifications to ensure that the customer’s specific requirements are met.
  6. Warehousing and Shipping: Once the final product is ready then it is either sent to warehouse or shipped to the customer’s address.
