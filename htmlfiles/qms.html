Incorporation

Company was incorporated as QMS Medical Allied Services Private Limited on September 14, 2017. Subsequently Company was converted into public limited company and the name of the company was changed to QMS Medical Allied Services Limited and the fresh certificate of incorporation consequent to conversion was issued on November 06, 2020 by the Registrar of Companies, Mumbai.

History

  • 2018-19: Crossed 50 crore turnover.
  • 2020-21: Crossed 100 crore turnover.

Holding and Subsidiary

  • Company does not have any holding and subsidiary company.

Business of the Company

  • Company is engaged in the marketing and distribution of a wide range of medical and healthcare devices and accessories.
  • Company market and distribute its products to various end users such as clinics, pharma, and medical companies and to individual users.
  • It has more than 600 SKUs which it sells to over 130 customers.
  • It gets its products manufactured in India from third party manufacturers, under its own brand.
  • It distributes its products through offline and online mode.
  • It provides online service through its website i.e., www.qmsmeds.com. to medical equipment dealers, distributors, surgeons, and clinicians for sourcing medical supplies from surgical supply store.

Product Portfolio

  1. Medical Devices: Blood Pressure Monitor, Glucose Monitoring Kits & Strips, Dental Products, Personal Protection Kits, Stethoscope, Oxygen Concentrator, pulse oximeter, IR thermometer, Anatomy set, Orthopedic Aids and Weighing Scales, Diagnostic Machines, ECG Machines, Nebulizer, Opthalmoscope, Otoscope, Skin Stepler.
  2. Medical Consumables: Disinfection, Filters, Infection prevention tools, Personal protection like PPE kits
  3. Miscellaneous products: Compression bandages, Wound management dressing kits, Dental products

Brand:

Company recently launches brand “Q-Devices” with an intention to provide affordable and quality products in the market to become a customer preferred medical device company.
