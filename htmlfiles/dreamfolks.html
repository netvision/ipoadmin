Incorporation

Company was originally incorporated as ‘Believe Tradelink Private Limited’, at New Delhi as a private limited company under the Companies Act, 1956 and received a certificate of incorporation issued by the RoC, on April 24, 2008. Subsequently Company’s name was changed to ‘Dreamfolks Services Private Limited’. Thereafter Company was converted into a public limited company and name of the company was changed to ‘Dreamfolks Services Limited’

Business of the Company

  • DreamFolks is a dominant player and India's largest airport service aggregator platform, an incubator of the industry with its unique, asset light, capital efficient business model.
  • Company provides the following airport related services:
    1. lounges access - includes 8 railway stations in India.
    2. food and beverage
    3. spa
    4. meet and assist
    5. airport transfer
    6. transit hotels /nap room access
    7. baggage transfer
  • It has 60 full time employees as of March 31, 2022.
  • Through its partnerships with other service providers, Company has global footprint extending to 1,416 Touch-points in 121 countries across the world out of which, 244 Touch-points are present in India and 1,172 Touch-points overseas.
  • It provides access to certain airport related services like lounges via the more traditional mechanisms like credit cards or debit cards, membership cards, or via digital solutions such as mobile applications using hybrid technology.
