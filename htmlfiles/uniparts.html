Incorporation

The company was incorporated as “Uniparts India Limited” under the Companies Act, 1956 at Delhi, pursuant to a certificate of incorporation dated September 26, 1994, issued by the RoC and received a certificate of commencement of business on September 7, 1998, from the RoC.

History

  • 2000:
    • Established the first facility in Noida, Uttar Pradesh.
    • Commenced sales to domestic OEMs in the agriculture sector in India and developed 3PL OEM product vertical.
  • 2001-2004:
    • Commenced sales to John Deere in India in 2001, in Spain in 2002, and in the USA in 2003.
    • Commenced sales to OEMs in Japan in 2004.
  • 2005
    • Consolidated various businesses into the Company including those of M/s Gripwel Fasteners, Farmparts Company, and SKG Engineering Private Limited.
    • Acquired a manufacturing and distribution facility in the USA, in Eldridge, Iowa.
    • Commenced sales to OEMs in the construction sector in the USA.
    • Set up a warehousing and distribution facility in Augusta, Georgia.
  • 2006
    • Commenced manufacturing products for the PMP vertical in India.
  • 2007
    • Acquired the balance equity stake in UOI.
    • Acquired 50% + 1 share equity interest in Kavee (a joint venture with Kramp), which owned 100% interest in Uniparts Kavee Services B.V.
    • Started manufacturing hydraulic cylinder components in one of the Noida facilities in Uttar Pradesh.
    • Consolidated the business of Sweaty Spirit Apparel Limited.
    • Awarded with ‘All India Award for Export Excellence.’
  • 2008
    • Commenced operations at the manufacturing facility located at Visakhapatnam, Andhra Pradesh.
    • Awarded a gold trophy for ‘Top Exporter of the year’ 2007-2008 by the Engineering Export Promotion Council.
  • 2010
    • Set up a warehousing and distribution facility in Hennef, Germany.
  • 2011
    • Started sales to the hydraulics market in the USA from the manufacturing facility located at Visakhapatnam, Andhra Pradesh.
  • 2012
    • Commenced sale of components for the European construction OEM market.
  • 2013
    • Sold entire equity interest in Kavee, with effect from April 1, 2012, to Kramp.
    • Won first prize at CII Kaizen Competition.
  • 2015
    • Ranked at 432 by the Fortune India Next 500.

    • Awarded with ‘Innovative use of Training and Development as an HR Initiative for OD’ by Global Training Development Leadership.

  • 2019

Holding and Subsidiary

  • Company has no holding company.
  • Company has four direct Subsidiaries as Gripwel Fasteners Private Limited (“GFPL”), Uniparts USA Limited (“UUL”), Uniparts India GmbH (“UIG”), Gripwel Conag Private Limited (“GCPL”).
  • Company has One Indirect Subsidiary Uniparts Olsen Inc. (“UOI”).

Business of the Company

  • It is a global manufacturer of engineered systems and solutions.
  • It is one of the leading suppliers of systems and components for the off-highway market in the agriculture and construction, forestry and mining (“CFM”), and aftermarket sectors.
  • It also caters to the aftermarket segment, especially for the 3PL product range.
  • It has a presence across over 25 countries.

Product Portfolio

  • 3-point linkage systems (“3PL”)
  • Precision machined parts (“PMP”)
  • Power take-off (“PTO”)
  • Fabrications
  • Hydraulic cylinders or their components.

Customers:

Company has a customer base of over 125 customers, diversified across industries and geographies.

Manufacturing, Warehousing, and Distribution Facilities

  • Manufacturing Facilities: Company has 6 (Six) Manufacturing facilities out of which five are located in India and One in the US.
  • Warehousing Facility: Company has 3 warehouses, Two in the US and one in Europe.
  • Distribution Facilities: It has One Distribution facility in India.
