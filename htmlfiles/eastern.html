Incorporation

The company was incorporated as "Oswal Infotech Private Limited", a private limited company under the Companies Act, 1956, pursuant to a certificate of incorporation dated July 28, 1995, issued by the Registrar of Companies, West Bengal. Subsequently, the name of the company was changed to 'Eastern Infoway Private Limited', Further, the company was converted into a public limited company and the name of the company was changed to 'Eastern Infoway Limited'. The company shifted its registered office with effect from December 07, 2002, from 4, Ganesh Chandra Avenue, Kolkata 700 013, West Bengal, India to 16, Ganesh Chandra Avenue, P.S. Hare Street, Kolkata 700 013, West Bengal, India. The name of the company was again changed to 'Eastern Logica Infoway Limited'. Thereafter, with effect from November 01, 2012, the registered office of the company shifted to 2, Saklat Place, 1st Floor, Kolkata-700072, West Bengal, India.

Business of the Company

  • The company is engaged in multi-brand retail and retail selling as well as distribution of branded smartphones, IT hardware, software, allied accessories and services.
  • The company also provides networking and security solutions to consumers.
  • Since the commencement of business, it has been steadily increasing market reach to cover 11 cities across the country by setting up retail stores, and distribution centres, venturing into e-commerce and setting up virtual offices.
  • As on December 15, 2022, the company has 4 offices, 11 retail stores, 2 distribution centres and virtual offices across 11 cities in the Country.
  • It primarily operates business activities across retail, distribution and e-commerce platforms of branded smartphones and IT hardware & peripherals.
  • There is a massive category of electronic products which includes Televisions, Cameras, Digital Cameras, Calculators, VCRs, DVDs, Clocks, Smartwatches, MacBooks, Audio Devices, Headphones and other home products.
  • It has also registered with the Government e-marketplace (GEM portal).
