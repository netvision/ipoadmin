Incorporation

Company was originally incorporated as a One Person Company under the name “Ekennis Software Service (OPC) Private Limited” under the Companies Act, 2013 and certificate of incorporation was issued by the Registrar of Companies, Central Registration Centre, Manesar on February 28, 2019. Thereafter, the status of the Company was changed to public limited and the name of the company was changed to “Ekennis Software Service Limited” and a fresh certificate of incorporation was issued on January 04, 2022 by the Registrar of Companies, Bangalore.

History

  • 2019 :
    • Recognized as a Start-Up by the DPIIT.
    • Company was awarded with the India ICON Awards, 2019 in the category of “Best IT Vocational Training Institute” in Karnataka organized by “blindwink”.
  • 2021 :
    • Purchase of Machineries to start Digital Printing, 3D Printing, and Packaging solution segment of the company.
    • Set up of Branch Offices at Bangalore and Jaipur for expansion of Digital Printing business.

Holdings and Subsidiaries:

  • Company does not have any holding, subsidiary or joint venture company.

Business of the Company

  • Company is in the business of Software IT solution and consultancy services, IT products / software development and training, Enterprise Resource Planning solution, e - learning management solution.
  • It is also engaged in providing 3D and digital printing & packaging, design management consultancy, etc in retail, food, fashion, sports, baby care and agriculture industries.
  • It also provides business process analysis and system design, technical expertise, implementation, training and support services.
  • It has expertise in SAP ERP (Technical & Functional).
  • Its major focus areas for implementation are discrete manufacturing, automotive, food processing, chemicals, and telecom sectors.
  • It also has 4.5 years of service experience in delivering Training, fill-stack web development, project handling, outsourcing & recruitment.
  • It has two branch offices located at Jaipur and Bangalore.

Human Resources:

  • As on December 31, 2021, company has total 54 employees.
  • It has a team of professionals and consultants who have the capabilities to support service and are trained and qualified on SAP, Microsoft and Oracle.
  • It also has outsourcing manpower delivering ERP Support services with the clients, having built-in software application packages to support various operations.
