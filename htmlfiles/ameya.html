Incorporation

Company was incorporated as Private Limited Company under the name “Ameya Precision Engineers Private Limited” vide Certificate of Incorporation dated December 06, 2012 issued by Registrar of Companies, Maharashtra, Pune. Subsequently company was converted into a public limited company and name of the company was changed to Ameya Precision Engineers Limited.

History

  • 2012 : Purchased Business of M/s Ameya Engineers via Slump Sales.
  • 2019 : ISO 9001:2015 in accordance with TUV Nord.
  • 2019 : Membership in Mahratta Chamber of Commerce, Industries and Agriculture, Pune.
  • 2020 : One Star Export House.

Holdings and Subsidiaries:

  • Company does not have any holding, subsidiary or joint venture company.

Business of the Company

  • It is engaged in manufacturing of high-end precision components (shafts, and other trim components) for the general engineering industry.
  • Its major customers are valve and pump manufacturers.
  • The company also offers specialized processes like hard-facing and overlay of wear and corrosion-resistant alloys in line with ASME procedures.
  • Company has a manufacturing facility situated at Pune – 412205, Maharashtra, India.
  • Company exports its products to the United States of America, United Kingdom, Germany, Singapore, Slovenija, Austria, and Brazil.
  • Its majority of the sales are through exports which contributed 94.07% for the financial year ended March 31, 2022.

Product Portfolio:

The Company is engaged in the manufacturing of:

  • Shafts/stems such as Gate Vavles, Butterfy vavles, Pumps
  • Flanges such as Valve Bonnets, Seats/ Discs
  • Plugs and Hard Facing and Corrosion Resistant Overlays such as Stellite / Colmonoy, Valve Seats /Discs / Plugs and Pump Wear Rings.
