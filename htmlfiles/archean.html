Incorporation

The company was originally formed as a partnership firm under the name of “Archean Chemical Industries” in Chennai, pursuant to a partnership deed dated November 20, 2003. Subsequently, the partnership firm was converted into a private limited company under the Companies Act, 1956 with the name “Archean Chemical Industries Private Limited” and a certificate of incorporation dated July 14, 2009, was issued by the Registrar of Companies, Tamil Nadu at Chennai. Subsequently, the company was converted into a public company and the name of the company was changed to “Archean Chemical Industries Limited”.

  • Company does not have any holding company, joint venture, or associate company.
  • Company has one wholly-owned Subsidiary, namely, Acume Chemicals Private Limited.

Business of the Company

  • It is a leading specialty marine chemical manufacturer in India and focused on producing and exporting bromine, industrial salt, and sulphate of potash to customers around the world.
  • It is the largest exporter of bromine and industrial salt by volume in India in Fiscal 2021 and has amongst the lowest cost of production globally in both bromine and industrial salt.
  • It produces its products from brine reserves in the Rann of Kutch, located on the coast of Gujarat.
  • Its marine chemicals business is predominately conducted on a business-to-business basis both in India and internationally.
  • It is the only manufacturer of sulphate of potash from natural sea brine in India.

Customers:

  • It marketed its products to 18 global customers in 13 countries and to 24 domestic customers.
  • Its customers include leading domestic and international multinational companies.
  • ts major customers include Sojitz Corporation, Shandong Tianyi Chemical Corporation, Unibrom Corporation, Wanhau Chemicals and Qatar Vinyl Company Limited.

Exports:

Key geographies to which the company exports its products include China, Japan, South Korea, Qatar, Belgium, and the Netherlands.

Manufacturing Facility

  • It manufactures its products at the facility near Hajipir in Gujarat.
  • Its manufacturing facility had an installed capacity of 28,500 MT per annum of bromine, 3,000,000 MT per annum of industrial salt and 130,000 MT per annum of sulphate of potash.
