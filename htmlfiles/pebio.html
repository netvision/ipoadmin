Incorporation

Company was incorporated as EP Bio composites Limited on January 17, 2020 under the Companies Act, 2013. Subsequently pursuant to business takeover agreement dated August 31, 2021 Company acquired the business of M/s. OM Ventures, a proprietorship concern of one of its Promoter Leena Kamat.

Holdings and Subsidiaries:

  • Company does not have any holding and subsidiary company.

Business of the Company

  • It is engaged into manufacturing and supply of fibre re-inforced polymers (FRP) products such as door shutters, frames, Biodigestor Tanks, Bio toilets and allied products.
  • FRP is an eco-friendly substitute for wood that has long been the chosen building material - a limited resource that has resulted in major environmental problems, such as fast forest decline.
  • The company focus on giving sanitation solutions to those who do not have access to toilets, as well as to those who have toilets but face trouble treating and managing the waste through septic tanks.
  • It has supplied more than 2,00,000 doors thereby saving thousands of trees and has supplied more than 2,500 Bio toilets to households helping in reduction of open defecation thereby contributing to clean and green surroundings.
  • It has clientele of more than 3500 people.
  • Presently, Company is operating in Goa and North Karnataka (Belgaum, Hubli & Dharward) through its Sales Representative, Retailers & Business Associates.
