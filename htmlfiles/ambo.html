Incorporation

The company was originally incorporated as a Private Limited Company in the name of “Sunny Biscuits Bakery and Confectionery Private Limited” on September 07, 1994. Subsequently, the name of the company was changed to “Ambo Foods Private Limited.” Subsequently, again, the name of the company was changed to “Ambo Agritec Private Limited.” The company was converted into a Public Limited Company under the provisions of the Companies Act, 2013, and the name of the Company was changed to “Ambo Agritec Limited.”

History

  • 2013: Company in addition to its existing business has forayed into sales through e-commerce.

Holding and Subsidiary

  • Company does not have any Holding, subsidiary, or Joint Venture Companies.

Business of the Company

  • Manufacturing of Biscuits (at own factory)
  • Outsourced Manufacturing of Edible Oil, Vanaspati, Bengali Speciality Products etc. (through a third party)
  • Trading of Crude Linseed Oil, Soyabean Meal, etc.
  • Its state-of-the-art manufacturing plant for biscuits is situated at Mangalpur Industrial Estate, Raniganj in the state of West Bengal, India.

Product Portfolio:

  • Biscuits: It includes various verity of biscuits such as Marie, Crackers, Glucose, Coconut, Butter, Cream, etc.
  • Edible Oil:
    • It includes Kachi Ghani Mustard Oil and Refined Rice Bran Oil
    • It has started selling oil on online platforms such as Flipkart, Big Basket, Reliance Retail, JioMart, etc.
  • Vanaspati:
    • Hydrogenated vegetable oil better known as "Vanaspati"
    • It is available in 15 Kgs Plastic Jars
    • It is marketed through Redistribution Channels, managed by the company sales force.
  • Bengali Speciality Products:
    • It is a quality staple food and pulses segment.
    • Its product ranges Includes Gobindobhog Rice, Posto Dana, Sona Moong Dal, Kasuni etc.

Brands:

Its products are marketed under its own brand names “Happy Bites” for Biscuits, “Happy Heart” for Edible Oil, “Ambo Gold” & “Vanaspati 2000” for Vanaspati, and “Max Health” for Bengali Speciality Products.

Properties:

  • Company does not own any property.
  • Its Registered Office, Manufacturing Facility, and Packaging Unit of Bengali Specialized Product are on lease.
