Incorporation

Company was incorporated as Eighty Jewellers Private Limited under the Companies Act, 1956. Thereafter, the Company was converted to public limited company vide special resolution passed by the shareholders of the Company on October 12, 2021 and the name of the Company was changed to “Eighty Jewellers Limited” vide fresh certificate of incorporation issued by the Registrar of Companies, Chhattisgarh on October 28, 2021.

History

  • 2013
    • Opening of Branch in Ahmedabad, Gujarat
  • 2014
    • Entering into Franchisee arrangements with Anopchand Tilokchand Jewellers Private Limited.
    • Arrangement with Sub-franchisee i.e., M/s. Chhattisgarh sales Corporation for opening of Showroom at Korba.
  • 2018
    • Started selling goods with M/s. Grand Bhagwati Store at Bilaspur under Anopchand Tilokchand name.
    • Opening of Branch in Andheri (W), Mumbai to enhance online trading business.
    • Discontinuation of the Branch at Ahmedabad, Gujarat.

Holdings and Subsidiaries:

  • Company does not have any holding, subsidiary and joint venture.

Business of the Company

  • It is currently engaged as a master franchisee of Anopchand Tilokchand Jewellers Private Limited (“ATJPL”).
  • It is involved in the trading of various jewelleries, ornaments, watches and luxury articles made out of gold, silver, diamonds and platinum studded with precious and semi-precious stones under the B2B model in the name “Anopchand Tilokchand Jewellers”.
  • Its product portfolio includes rings, earrings, armlet, pendants, gajrahs, nose rings, bracelets, chains, necklaces, bangles, watches, luxury items and other wedding jewellery.
  • Company sells its products to a sub-franchisee store at Korba, Chhattisgarh and a retail store at Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh.
  • The Company deals only in jewellery certified by BIS Hallmark.
