Incorporation

Company was originally incorporated as ―Global Long-life Hospital and Research Private Limited ‘‘as a private limited company under the provisions of Companies Act, 1956. Subsequently, Company was converted in to a public limited Company pursuant to a special resolution passed by shareholders at the EGM held on August 05, 2021 and consequently the name of Company was changed to ―Global Long-life Hospital and Research Limited and a fresh certificate of incorporation was issued by the Registrar of Companies, Ahmedabad dated August 31,2021

Business of the Company

  • Global Longlife Hospital and Research Limited provides health care services.
  • The company offers Oncology, Ophthalmology, Cardiology, Dermatology, Gynecology, Spine Surgery, Plastic Surgery, Ultrasound, Microbiology, Dialysis, and Physiotherapy among other healthcare services.
  • Company is known as a Multi-Speciality Tertiary Care Hospital of Gujarat with 110 beds well equipped for Medical & Surgical Specialities.
  • Company has 11 full time consultants, and have team of more than 30 experienced doctors as a consultant.
  • Staff strength of the company also comprises 37 nursing staff and more than 50 paramedical, corporate and support staff and pharmacists as on December 31, 2021.
  • It provides platform to the doctors for treatment of their patients.
  • As a policy any doctor having experience of more than 3 years can admit his patient in Global Longlife hospital.
  • It is certified by National Accreditation Board for Hospitals and Healthcare Providers (“NABH Accredited”).
  • Through the Mukhya Mantri Amrutum Yojana it has served over 6,000 patients up till now.
