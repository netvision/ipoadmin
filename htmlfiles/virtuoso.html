Incorporation

Company was originally incorporated as Virtuoso Optoelectronics Private Limited at Nashik, Maharashtra. Subsequently, Company was converted into a Public Limited Company and name of the company was changed to Virtuoso Optoelectronics Limited vide a fresh certificate of incorporation dated April 19, 2021 issued by Registrar of Companies, Mumbai.

History

  • 2016 :
    • Started manufacturing of Electronics Products and Lighting Products by setting up in-house EMS Facility in a 6,000 sq. ft facility.
  • 2017 :
    • VOEPL received its ISO 9001 certification.
  • 2018:
    • VOEPL took its first steps into White Goods Manufacturing by laying the foundation for manufacturing of Air Conditioners (ACs).
  • 2019 :
    • VOEPL was selected to be part of the Stanford Seed Transformation Program for SMEs by Graduate School of Business (GSB), Stanford University, USA.
  • 2021 :
    • Expansion of the White Goods Division (AC) and moved into its new 100K sq. ft. facility

Holdings and Subsidiaries:

  • Company does not have any holding, subsidiary or joint venture company.

Business of the Company

  • Company manufacture consumer durable goods and assemble a wide array of products and provide end-to-end product solutions.
  • It serves under both original equipment manufacturer ("OEM") and original design manufacturer ("ODM") business models.
  • Under the OEM model, it manufactures and supply products basis designs developed by the customer.
  • Under the ODM model, in addition to manufacturing, it conceptualizes and design the products.
  • Its key customers include leading brands in Air Conditioning & Water Heater segments.
  • It has over 5 years of experience in manufacturing sector.
  • It has two manufacturing facilities which are strategically located in and around Nashik (Maharashtra).

Products Portfolio:

  1. Split Air Conditioners
  2. Water Heaters
  3. LED Lighting products
  4. Other miscellaneous products such as injection molding components for air conditioners.

Property Details:

Registered office and both the manufacturing facilities are on lease.
