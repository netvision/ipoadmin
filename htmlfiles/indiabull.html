Incorporation

Company was originally incorporated as “IT Indiabull Private Limited” on September 02, 2016. Subsequently, the name was changed to “Naturo Indiabull Private Limited”. Subsequently Company was converted into Public Limited Company vide a fresh certificate of incorporation issued by Registrar of Companies- Jaipur and name of the company was changed to “Naturo Indiabull Limited”.

History

  • 2019 :
    • Company entered into FMCG Business.

Holdings and Subsidiaries:

  • Company does not have any holding, subsidiary or joint venture company.

Business of the Company

  • It is in the business of the Fast-Moving Consumer Goods industry.
  • Company is engaged to carry on the business of garments, textiles, jewellery, FMCG, Real Estate, Stone-Mines, Herbal Products, Automobile, Printing, Share Trading, providing education, Agriculture Seeds and Fertilizers, Hospitality and Food items.
  • Company is in the business of trading of various health products and kits including Sanitary Towels, Sanitary Napkins, hand sanitiser and other health kits etc.
  • Company is diversifying its products to various health and herbal products like, juices, soaps, shampoos, medicines, tablets, etc.
  • Company sells its products in its own brand both directly to wholesalers and also through agents.
  • Company’s areas of operations are Jaipur, Rajasthan.

Products of the Company:

  • Women Healthcare Products: V Care, Bapy Wipes, Nipple Shield, Sanitary Napkin etc.
  • Healthcare Products: Aloe Vera Juice, Amla Juice, Giloy Tulsi Papaya Juice, Noni Gold Juice, Aloe Vera Charcoal Soap, Aloe Vera Lavender Soap, Aloe Vera Jasmin Soap, Hand Sanitizer, Anti Addiction Drop, Highly Concentrate Mineral Drop, Tulsi Drop, Aloe Vera Rose Skin Gel, Red Aloe Vera Gel etc.
