Incorporation

The company was originally incorporated as “RBM Infracon Private Limited” as a Private Limited Company under the provisions of the Companies Act, 1956 vide Certificate of Incorporation dated April 01, 2013, issued by the Registrar of Companies, Uttar Pradesh. Subsequently, the company was converted into a Public Limited Company pursuant to the shareholders’ resolution passed at the Extra-ordinary General Meeting of the Company held on September 23, 2022, and the name of the company was changed to “RBM Infracon Limited”. A fresh Certificate of Incorporation consequent upon Conversion from a Private Limited Company to a Public Limited Company dated October 10, 2022, was issued by the Registrar of Companies, Uttar Pradesh.

Business of the Company

  • The company is engaged in the business of engineering, execution, testing, commissioning operation, and maintenance, particularly in the field of mechanical and rotary equipment for Oil & Gas Refineries, Gas Cracker Plants, Coal/Gas/WHR based Power Plants, Petrochemical, Chemicals, Cement, Fertilizers.
  • The company is a Class -I certified Boiler Repairer / Erector and System /Feed Line fabricator / Erector service provider under Indian Boiler Regulations- 1950.
  • The company provides the following services in different plants like crude (CDU-VDU), DHDS, FCCU, NHT-CCR, HMU-I & II, VGO –DHDT-ISOM, CPP (Boiler, Piping HT job with IBR coordination), DCU(Coker) Utility, off-site, RTF (Pit area) dispatch, Rail and Jetty with around 650 Manpower and involve in ARC maintenance job in RIL-Jamnagar and YARA-Babrala.
  • It has executed around 14 Turnaround Shutdowns Job including Mega/Mini Shutdowns in Reliance Industries Limited, Nayara Energy Limited (Formerly Known as Essar Oil Limited) and Yara Fertilisers India Limited & HMEL-Bathinda.
  • It has a work order book of approximately ₹121 Crores (Including GST) as on September 06, 2022, and Recently received a letter of Intent from Malco Energy Limited worth ₹ 200 Crores for Operations and Maintenance of Metcoke Manufacturing Plant.
  • It secures contracts generally through one-to-one negotiation and most of the contracts come from existing clients, the pricing of services is determined based on the type of work and estimated duration within which it needs to be completed as well as the type of equipment required.
  • The prices are determined by negotiating directly with the client. In general, services are provided to the customers on credit and it maintains an ongoing account of receivables from customers.
  • It has a total of 2049 Human Resources.
