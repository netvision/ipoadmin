Incorporation

Company was originally incorporated as a private company in name and style of Kesar Impex (India) Private Limited under the Companies Act, 1956 and a certificate of incorporation dated November 05, 2003 issued by Assistant Registrar of Companies, Mumbai, Maharashtra. Subsequently, company was converted into a public limited company and the name of the Company was changed to “Kesar India Limited” and a fresh certificate of incorporation dated December 15, 2021 was issued by the RoC, Mumbai, Maharashtra.

Business of the Company (As of December 31, 2021)

  • Company is engaged in undertaking development & construction of real estate projects.
  • It primarily operates in Nagpur.
  • It mainly focuses on developing projects on land or sell plotted lands held by it as stock-in-trade.
  • It is a flagship company of Kesar Group, which markets its projects under the brand name of “Kesar Lands” or carries a prefix “Kesar”.
  • Its projects include residential & commercial plotting, residential, commercial and industrial buildings, houses and apartments, colonies, mills and factory's buildings, workshop's building, etc.
  • It is a member of esteemed associations such as Credai, Indian Green Building Council (IGBC) & also possess ISO Certification.
  • It has 23 permanent employees.
  • It is having a development potential of 2,124,654 sq. ft of land located at Nagpur.

Scope of operations:

Company’s operations cover all aspects of real estate development including –

  • The planning, execution and marketing of projects.
  • Construction and development of projects
