Incorporation

The company was originally incorporated as a private limited company under the Companies Act, 1956 pursuant to a certificate of incorporation issued by the Registrar of Companies, Maharashtra dated June 16, 1994, with the name ‘Ducol Organics & Colours Private Limited.’ Subsequently, the Company was converted into a public limited company and the name of the Company was changed to ‘Ducol Organics and Colours Limited.’

History

  • 1995:
    • Setting up of Unit I at Taloja.
  • 2010:
    • Setting up of Unit II at Taloja.
    • Unit II Phase masterbatch division acquired and production started.
  • 2022:
    • Setting up of Unit III at Mahad.

Holdings and Subsidiaries:

  • The company does not have any Holding, any subsidiary, or any associate and joint venture Companies.

Business of the Company

  • Company offers a range of pigment dispersions for various industries like Paints, Ink, Textile, Detergent, Paper, Rubber, and Plastics. color solutions for various industries like paints, ink, textile, rubber, plastic, leather, paper, soaps, detergents, and FMCG.
  • Company is engaged in the business of manufacturing and selling pigment dispersions, preparations, concentrates, paste colorants, and masterbatches.
  • It has a pan-India customer base, with integrated operations which involve the development, manufacturing, marketing, and distribution of a wide range of dispersions & masterbatches.

It offers the following pigment dispersions and preparations:

  1. Dutint – water-based dispersions for decorative paints.
  2. Duplast - MB for the plastic industry
  3. Duprint – water-based dispersion for printing ink formulations
  4. Dutex – pigment pastes for the textile printing industry
  5. Dusperse – for the soap and detergent industry
  6. Dupralin – for the paper industry
  7. Dupren – used in the rubber industry
  8. Ducem – Dry dispersions of various general applications

Manufacturing Units

  • The Company has three (3) manufacturing units situated near Mumbai out of which two (2) are situated at Taloja MIDC and the third is situated at Mahad MIDC.
  • It has obtained ISO 9001: 2015 certification for Unit I and Unit II situated at Taluja MIDC.

Properties
