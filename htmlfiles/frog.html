Incorporation

Company was incorporated on July 12, 2004 as a private limited company under the provisions of Companies Act, 1956 with the Registrar of Companies, Delhi in the name and style of ‘Frog Cellsat Private Limited’. Subsequently, Company was converted into public limited company pursuant to which the name of the Company was changed to “Frog Cellsat Limited”.

History

  • 2014 :
    • Company got listed on ITP Platform of National Stock Exchange of India Limited (NSE).
  • 2019 :
    • Recognized as Great Place to Work with ranking as 12th best among all India Mid-sized companies.
  • 2022 :
    • Company was assigned Credit Rating of ‘BWA SME 1’ (indicates ‘Highest’ level of credit worthiness adjudged in relation to other SMEs) by Brickworks.

Holding and Subsidiary

  • Company does not have any holding company and joint ventures.
  • Company has three subsidiaries namely Frog Tele Private Limited, Frog Services Private Limited, and Shiva Profiles Private Limited.

Business of the Company

  • It is engaged in manufacturing of telecom equipment’s used by telecom operator’s especially in Telecom Towers.
  • It manufactures 2G/3G/4G Multi-band Digital RF Repeaters, Multi-band Frequency Shift Repeater, Multi-band Optical DAS system, relative software, and accessories.
  • Company directly sale its products in the market and are not dependent upon any dealers/distributors for sale of products.
  • Company is providing its products and services across Asia, Europe, Africa, and the Middle East.
  • It has two production unit one is in Noida and second one is in located in Dehradun.

Products:

  • Digital Repeater
  • Optical DAS
  • Jumpers
  • Common Public Radio Interface (CPRI)
  • Remote electrical Tilt (RET) Cable

Services:

Services include all kinds of services related to Installation & Commissioning and Maintenance of BTS Cell Site and IBS site across India.
