Incorporation

The company was originally incorporated as “Abans Vanijya Private Limited” on September 24, 2009, in Kolkata, West Bengal as a private limited company under the Companies Act, 1956. Subsequently, the name of the Company was changed to “Abans Holdings Private Limited.” Further, Company was converted into a Public Limited Company and the name of the Company was changed to Abans Holdings Limited.

Business of the Company

  • Abans group is a globally diversified organization engaged in Financial Services, Gold Refining, Jewellery, Commodities Trading, Agricultural Trading and Warehousing, Software Development, and Real Estate.
  • Company is engaged in providing NBFC services, global institutional trading in equities, commodities, and foreign exchange, private client stock broking, depositary services, asset management services, investment advisory services, and wealth management services to corporate, institutional, and high net worth clients.
  • Company currently has active businesses in six (6) countries across the Eastern Continents, including Hongkong, the UK, UAE, China, Mauritius, and India.
  • Company represents the financial services arm of the Abans Group.
  • It also engaged in the business of warehousing, agri-trading, and payment gateway services through its subsidiaries, namely, Abans Agri Warehousing & Logistics Pvt. Ltd., Shanghai Yilan Trading Co. Ltd. (China) and Corporate Avenue Services Ltd.

Business is mainly organised as under:

  • Finance Business:
    • It operates an RBI Registered NBFC (Non-Deposit taking).
    • The finance business is primarily focused on lending to private traders and other small and medium businesses involved in the commodities trading market.
  • Agency Business:
    • It is SEBI registered Stock and Commodity Exchange Broker with memberships across all the major stock exchanges in India, including BSE, NSE, MSEI, MCX, NCDEX, and ICEX.
    • It offers various institutional and non-institutional trading services, wealth management, and private client brokerage services, mainly in equity, commodities and foreign exchange.
  • Capital and other Business:
    • Capital Business includes internal treasury operations which manage excess capital funds.
    • It also provides Warehousing Services to commodity market participants.
