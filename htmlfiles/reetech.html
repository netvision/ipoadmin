Incorporation

The Company was originally incorporated as a Private Limited Company under the name of “M R Agriculture Private Limited.” Further, pursuant to a Special Resolution of Shareholders passed in the Extra Ordinary General Meeting held on June 15, 2020 name of the Company was changed to “Reetech International Cargo and Courier Private Limited” and a Fresh Certificate of Incorporation was issued by Registrar of Companies, Chhattisgarh. Subsequently, Company was converted to Public Limited Company and the name of the Company was changed to “Reetech International Cargo and Courier Limited.”

History

  • 2020 : Started Coal Trading and supplying coal all over India.
  • 2022 : Company achieved a turnover of Rs. 100+ crores.

Holding and Subsidiary

  • The company has no holding, subsidiary, or joint venture.
  • However, the company has one associate company as M Ahuja Project (India) Private Limited.

Business of the Company

  • Company started its operation in the year 2009-10 Initially, the Company was engaged in the business of agricultural goods such as Rice Milling, Rice Sorting, Dal Milling, Dal Processing, and Agriculture Products.
  • The business of trading Agriculture goods had been operated by the Company successfully in the past.
  • In the year 2020, the promoters commenced the business of trading of coal.
  • An effective handling and transportation of the orders are facilitated through Road transportation and Railway.
  • It procures Indian coal through e-auctions conducted by the various subsidiaries of Coal India Limited.
  • It imports US coal on Kandla port of GCV higher than 7000.
  • In a very short span of time, the Company has been able to recognize itself as a trusted name in the coal industry due to its commitment to quality and reliability since its inception.
  • Company is one of the recognized traders of Coal and performs its operations from Gangavaram and another Port.

Products Portfolio

  • South African Coal
  • Indonesian Coal
  • Indian Coal
  • US Coal

Customers

The company supplies its products to customers in various sectors i.e., Power, Steel, Rolling, and other industries.

Mode of transportation

  1. By Sea: Purchase Coal while it is still in the process of being imported. A lot less paperwork and early ownership of stock results in reduced cost for the buyer.
  2. By Rail: Use of rail to deliver the coal to its customers. This mode is subject to availability and company policy.
  3. By Road: Under this mode coal is delivered through trucks and trolley. For Immediate demands, this is the best mode of purchase.

Property Details:

  • Owned Property: Company does not own any property.
  • Leased Property: Registered Office is taken on lease in Chhattisgarh, India at Rs. 10,000 per month from June 01, 2022, to May 31, 2023.

Litigations:

  • Litigation against Company:
    • 3 outstanding cases having an amount in dispute of Rs. 0.58 lakhs of litigation involving tax matters.
  • Litigation against Promoters:
    • 12 outstanding cases having an amount in dispute of Rs. 13.37 lakhs of litigation involving tax matters.
  • Litigation against Group Companies:

  • Litigation against Directors:
