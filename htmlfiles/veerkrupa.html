Incorporation

Company was originally incorporated as Veerkrupa Jewellers Private Limited on September 13, 2019 under the Companies Act, 2013 and a certificate of incorporation was issued by the Registrar of Companies, Gujarat, Dadra and Nagar Haveli. Subsequently, company was converted into a public limited company and the name of the company was changed to Veerkrupa Jewellers Limited and a fresh certificate of incorporation dated January 17, 2020 was issued by the RoC, Gujarat, Dadra and Nagar Haveli.

History

  • 2019
    • Entered into business succession agreement dated January 01, 2020 with M/S Veerkrupa Jewellers.
  • 2020
    • Company converted into Public Limited from Private Limited.

Business of the Company

  • Company is engaged in the jewellery business.
  • Its business includes sale of jewellery made of gold, silver, studded and other jewellery products such as diamond, platinum and other precious and semi-precious stones.
  • Its product profile includes designer, traditional, modern and combined designs of jewellery at best prices.
  • It has total 3 showrooms including one in Naroda and other two in Narol, Ahmedabad.
  • It procures jewellery from jewellery manufacturers‘l ocated at Ahmedabad, Mumbai, Rajkot and Surat.
  • It also procures the old gold jewellery items which are exchanged by the customers towards new jewellery items.
  • It also get jewellery manufactured through independent job workers.
  • Company's all jewelleries are BIS hallmarked.

Products

  • Company's product profile includes earrings, chains, bracelets, gold/silver bars, Bands, Mandalsutra, Pendant sets and chains, Anklets, Waist Belts and necklaces.
  • Company also offers Hand tools, pooja items, and decorative items in silver.
  • It essentially sell products for different occasions including special occasions, such as weddings and party-wear, jewellery for personal milestones, festival jewellery, daily-wear jewellery, kid‘s jewellery and men‘s jewellery.
