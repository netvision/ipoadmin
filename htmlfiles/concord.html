Incorporation

The company was originally incorporated as a Private Limited Company under the name “Concord Control Systems Private Limited” on January 19, 2011. Subsequently, Company was converted into a Public Limited Company and consequently, the name of the Company was changed to “Concord Control Systems Limited” and a fresh certificate of incorporation dated August 26, 2022, issued by the Registrar of Companies, Kanpur, Uttar Pradesh.

History

  • 2013
    • Commencement of Factory Unit I at UPSIDC Industrial Area, Lucknow, UP
  • 2017-18
    • Crossed Revenue of ₹ 10 Cr.
  • 2019-20
    • Acquired 66.67% stake in Drivetrain Solutions Private Limited.

  • 2022
    • The company has acquired a 24.98% stake in Rangetech Systems India Private Limited.

Holding and Subsidiary

The company does not have any Holdings, Associate Companies, and Joint Ventures but the company have 2 (two) Subsidiary Companies as Drivetrain Solutions Private Limited and Rangetech Systems India Private Limited.

Business of the Company

  • It is a Research Design and Standards Organisation (“RDSO”) approved vendor.
  • They are engaged in the business of manufacturing and supply of coach-related and electrification products for Indian Railways and other Railway Contractors.
  • They manufacture products required in railway coaches like Inter-Vehicular Coupler, Emergency Lighting Systems, Brushless DC carriage fan, Exhaust fans, Cable Jackets, Bellows, etc.
  • They also manufacture products required in the electrification of coaches and the broad-gauge network of Indian Railways like Battery Charger 200 AH, Battery Chargers 40 AH, and Tensile Testing Machine.

Manufacturing Facilities

It has two manufacturing units situated at Lucknow, Uttar Pradesh with a total size admeasuring to over 1880 sq. mtrs.

Clients

  • Some of the Govt Customers include Rail Vikas Nigam Limited, Bharat Heavy Electricals Limited, Delhi Metro Rail Corporation and Rail India Technical and Economic Service Limited (Rites)
  • Some of the Private Customers Include Larsen & Toubro Limited, Sterling and Wilson Pvt. Ltd, R.S. Infraprojects Private Limited and KEC International Limited.
