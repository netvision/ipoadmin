Incorporation

Company was incorporated on June 4, 2003, as ‘Prudent Corporate Advisory Services Limited’, a public limited company under the Companies Act, 1956. Company commenced its operations pursuant to a certificate for commencement of business dated June 20, 2003, issued by the RoC.

Holding and Subsidiary:

  • Company does not have a holding, associates and joint ventures.
  • Company has three subsidiaries namely Prudent Broking Services Private Limited (“PBSPL”), Prutech Financial Services Private Limited (“Prutech”), Gennext Insurance Brokers Private Limited (“Gennext”).

Business of the Company

  • It is an independent retail wealth management services group in India.
  • They are amongst the top mutual fund distributors in terms of average assets under management (“AAUM”) and commission received.
  • It offers a technology enabled, comprehensive investment and financial services platform with end-to-end solutions critical for financial products distribution and presence across both online and offline channels.
  • It provided wealth management services to 13,51,274 unique retail investors through 23,262 MFDs on business-to-business-to-consumer (“B2B2C”) platform and are spread across branches in 110 locations in 20 states in India, as on December 31, 2021.
  • It also distributes life and general insurance products in India through its wholly owned subsidiary, Gennext.

Products Offering

  • Mutual fund - FundzBazar that offers variety of investment products and company also offers a PrudentConnect technology platform which is like Virtual Office for mutual fund distributors (MFDs) registered with them.
  • Insurance Broking - Its Policyworld online platform offers a variety of insurance solutions.
  • Stock Broking - It offers two platforms namely WiseBasket (This offers Online model stock portfolios) and PruBazar (Online platform for trading in equities)
  • Other financial products - Other products such as PMS, AIFs, FDs, bonds, unlisted equities, loans against securities, NPS, structured products etc.
