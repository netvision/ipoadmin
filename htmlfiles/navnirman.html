Incorporation

Company was originally incorporated as a Limited Liability Partnership in the name and style of Modi‘s Navnirman LLP on December 31, 2015. Subsequently, it was converted into a public limited company on March 04, 2022 under the Companies Act, 2013 and a certificate of incorporation was issued by the Registrar of Companies, Maharashtra at Mumbai.

History

  • 2017-18
    • Received commencement certificate and began construction for the project Rashmi Sheela and Rashmi Sadhna.
  • 2019-20
    • Acquired 100% stake in Shree Modi’s Navnirman Pvt. Ltd. & thereby making it subsidiary.
    • Received Completion Certificate for the project Rashmi Sheela and Rashmi Sadhna.
  • 2021
    • Received Commencement Certificate for the project Rashmi Jewel, Rashmi Enclave and Rashmi Terrace through wholly owned subsidiary SMNPL.
  • 2022
    • Received Commencement Certificate Project Rashmi Kavita through wholly owned subsidiary SMNPL.

Holding and Subsidiary:

  • Company does not have any holding or joint venture. It has one subsidiary company, i.e., Shree Modi‘s Navnirman Private Limited (SMNPL).

Business of the Company

  • Company is a real estate development company primarily operating in Mumbai.
  • It covers key segments of the real estate market, namely: (i) residential and (ii) residential-cum- commercial.
  • It has a great understanding of the real estate market in Suburbs area of Mumbai.
  • It has a diversified portfolio of completed, ongoing and upcoming projects in mixed-use or single segment developments.
  • Company is transitioning to a real estate development model with a shift towards an asset light business strategy by doing Development Agreements.
  • Company also partner with top architects and design teams for project designing.
