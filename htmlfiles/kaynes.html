Incorporation

The company was incorporated on March 28, 2008, in Mysore, Karnataka as a private limited company under the Companies Act, 1956, with the name ‘Kaynes Technology India Private Limited.’ Subsequently, the name of the Company was changed to ‘Kaynes Technology India Limited’ upon conversion of the Company into a public company. and the fresh certificate of incorporation was issued by the RoC consequent upon the change of name on conversion to a public limited company on March 31, 2022.

Holding and Subsidiary

  • Company does not have a holding company.
  • Company has five Subsidiaries including one foreign subsidiary.
  • One of the Subsidiaries, Kaynes International Design & Manufacturing Private Limited, has been incorporated pursuant to a joint venture agreement and Company has entered into a joint venture agreement with Radha Madhav Inc.

Business of the Company

  • It is an end-to-end and IoT solutions-enabled integrated electronics manufacturer.
  • It has capabilities across the entire spectrum of ESDM services.
  • It is engaged in providing conceptual design, process engineering, integrated manufacturing, and life-cycle support for major players in the automotive, industrial, aerospace and defense, outer-space, nuclear, medical, railways, Internet of Things, Information Technology, and other segments.
  • It was one of the first companies to offer design-led electronics manufacturing to OEMs.
  • Company served to 346 customers in 21 countries as of March 31, 2022.

Business Verticals:

  • OEM – Turnkey Solutions – Box Build (“OEM – Box Build”)
  • OEM – Turnkey Solutions – Printed Circuit Board Assemblies (“PCBAs”) (“OEM – Turnkey Solutions”)
  • ODM: It offers ODM services in smart metering technology, smart street lighting, brush less DC (“BLDC”) technology.
  • Product Engineering and IoT Solutions: It offers conceptual design and product engineering services in industrial and consumer segments.

Manufacturing Facility

  • Company operates eight strategically located manufacturing facilities across India in the states of Karnataka, Haryana, Himachal Pradesh, Tamil Nadu, and Uttarakhand.
  • It also has Service centers at Cochin and Mumbai, catering to Railway, Aerospace, Defence and Industrial Clients.
