Incorporation

The company was incorporated as Rex Sealing and Packing Industries Private Limited on August 10, 2005, under the Companies Act, 1956 with the Registrar of Companies, Mumbai, Maharashtra. The status of the Company was changed to Public Limited and the name of the Company was changed to Rex Sealing and Packaging Industries Limited vide Special Resolution dated April 07, 2022. The fresh certificate of incorporation consequent to conversion was issued on August 01, 2022, by the Registrar of Companies, Mumbai, Maharashtra.

History

  • 2008:
    • Commenced Manufacturing of Gaskets at the Manufacturing Unit located at Taloja Industrial Area.
  • 2009:
    • Commenced Manufacturing of Fabric Expansion Joints at the Manufacturing Unit located at Taloja Industrial Area.
  • 2018:
    • Turnover of the Company crossed ₹ 20 crore mark.

Holdings and Subsidiaries:

  • The company does not have any Holding, any subsidiary, or any associate and joint venture Companies.

Business of the Company

  • Company is an ISO 9001 & 14001 Certified company.
  • It is a manufacturer and exporter of Sealing packing and Insulation products.
  • Its products are used by some well-known industrial houses in the Petrochemical, Pharmaceutical, Power, Steel, Cement, Chemical, Gas, and Process industry.
  • The principal raw materials used by the Company are Rubber, Fiber, Filler, Yarn, Ceramic Blankets, Stainless Steel Wire Mesh, Temperature Resistance Fabrics, and Polytetrafluoroethylene Foil.
  • It caters to both, domestic as well as international markets, limited to Gulf and Middle East countries.
  • It manufactures all types of “Asbestos Free” Gasket sheets for Automotive, Steel, and other processing units.
