Incorporation

The company was originally incorporated as ‘Arham Techonologies Private Limited’ on December 27, 2013, under the provision of the Companies Act, 1956. Subsequently, the name of the company changed to ‘Arham Technologies Private Limited.’ Subsequently, the company was converted into Public Limited and the name of the Company was changed to “Arham Technologies Limited” vide a fresh Certificate of Incorporation issued by the Registrar of Companies.

History

  • 2017
    • Acquired land in the Electronic Manufacturing Cluster, Raipur, Chhattisgarh from CSIDCL on a lease basis for setting up a manufacturing unit of Electronics Products.
  • 2019
    • Started its own manufacturing unit of LED Television with a production capacity of 36,000 nos. of Television per year.
  • 2021
    • Increased Production Capacity of 60,000 nos. of Television per year.
  • 2022
    • Increased Production Capacity of 84,000 nos. of Television per year.
    • Crossed Turnover of ₹ 30.00 crores.
    • Setting up of own fan manufacturing unit at the existing location and a term loan of ₹ 3.00 crores has been sanctioned by Bank of Baroda.

Holding and Subsidiary

  • The company does not have a holding, Subsidiary, Joint Venture, and Associate company.

Business of the Company

  • Company is engaged in manufacturing LED Smart Televisions, with different screen sizes.
  • It also manufactures Fans, Air Coolers, and Mixer Grinders through third-party manufacturers.
  • It also serves under Original Equipment Manufacturer (“OEM”) business models for LED Televisions.
  • Raw materials like open cell, Electronic Circuit (Mainboard), Cabinet, and Back Light Units (BLU) are imported from China and Hongkong due to pricing and other favourable factors.
  • It also does white labelling for various regional brands.
  • It has also launched a “D2C” website www.starshine.co.in for its products.
  • It also lists its products on e-commerce platforms i.e., Amazon.
  • Arham Technologies is strategically located in Electronic Manufacturing Cluster, in the new Smart City of Raipur, which is one of the very first smart cities in India.

Brands:

The company sales it’s products under the brand ‘STARSHINE’
