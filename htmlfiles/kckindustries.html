Incorporation

Company was originally incorporated at Chandigarh as “KCK Sales Private Limited” on 19th March, 2013 under the Companies Act, 1956 and the certificate of incorporation was issued by the Registrar of Companies, Punjab and Chandigarh. Subsequently, the company was converted into a public limited company and the name of the company was changed to “KCK Sales Limited” and a fresh certificate of incorporation dated 19st October, 2020 was issued by the Registrar of Companies, Chandigarh. Thereafter, the name of Company was changed to “KCK Industries Limited” on 11th November, 2020.

Histroy

  • 2020 :

    • Change of Object clause of the Company.
  • 2021 :
    • Starting of New Business Segment of Milling and trading of Rice.

Holdings and Subsidiaries:

  • Company does not have any holding, subsidiary or joint venture company.

Business of the Company

  • Company is engaged in the trading and distribution of combed and carded cotton yarns ranging from Ne 4 to Ne 40 in single and multifold and knitted fabrics.
  • It is also engaged in trading and distribution of high quality chemicals and dyes for textiles industry, leather, and paper industries.
  • It also deals in construction chemicals.
  • It also process and sell basmati and non-basmati rice.
  • It caters to various industries viz. Dyestuff and Dye intermediates, Textiles, apparel and garment industry, Speciality Chemicals etc.
  • It caters rice in North India like Delhi, Punjab, Gujarat & Haryana, Uttar Pradesh, etc.

Products

  1. Yarn:
    • It includes combed cotton yarn and carded cotton yarn.
    • Yarns are used for applications such as apparels, undergarments, Terry Towels, Denims, Medical Fabrics, Furnishing Fabrics and Industrial Fabrics.
  2. Chemicals and Dyes:
    • It includes textile auxillaries and dyeing chemicals.
    • It also includes auxiliaries to speciality chemicals used for dyeing, Finishing, Flame Retardant, optical Brightener, Pigment printing, Pre-treatment, Printing (other), Softener, Antifoaming/Deaerating, Detergent (pre-Treatment), Basic chemicals, etc.
  3. Rice:
    • It includes milled raw basmati rice and semi - milled rice
  4. Rice Husk

Raw Materials

  1. Traded Goods:
    • The raw material used is cotton yarn.
    • Company mostly procures cotton Yarn domestically from surrounding areas viz., Punjab.
  2. Rice:
    • The raw material for the Rice-mill is Paddy.
    • It is cultivated and procured from the local vendors.

Manufacturing Facilities

  • Company’s manufacturing plant is located at Sunam Lehra Road Village Khokhar, Distt Sangrur.
  • At processing plant, company processes paddy to basmati and non-basmati rice.
  • The plant is fully integrated and automatic.
  • It is equipped with requisite utilities and modern infrastructure facilities including power and water.
  • The Capacity of plant is 12 Ton/ hour and current utilization is 7 Ton/ Hour.
