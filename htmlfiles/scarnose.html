Incorporation

Company was originally incorporated as a Private Limited Company in the name of “Chordia Agro Products Private Limited” on April 12, 2011 under the provisions of the Companies Act, 1956. Subsequently, name of the company changed to “Scarnose International Private Limited” vide a fresh certificate of incorporation dated July 26, 2018 issued by Registrar of Companies – Ahmedabad. Subsequently company was converted into Public Limited Company under the provisions of the Companies Act, 2013 and the name of the Company was changed to “Scarnose International Limited”

Business of the Company

  • Company is engaged in the trading of Nutrition feeds supplement made of Cereals like wheat, bazra etc.
  • Company also engaged in the trading of Raw Cotton, Cotton Bales, Yarn from the year 2020.
  • It also diversifies into manufacturing of Ladies Kurtis and Dress Materials through outsourcing model from the year 2021 and selling them under their domestic brand "Scarnose".
  • Garments are made using printed fabric and solid dyed fabrics.
  • Most of the garments are supplied with extensive value addition involving embroidery, hand embroidery and hand work including beads, sequences.
  • Company place orders for raw material requirements from variety of suppliers which comprise of finished fabric, accessories such as fasteners, buttons, labels, laces and other consumables.
