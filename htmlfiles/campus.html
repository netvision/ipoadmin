Incorporation

Company was incorporated as ‘Action Renewable Energy Private Limited’ pursuant to a certificate of incorporation dated September 24, 2008, issued by the RoC. Thereafter, pursuant to a resolution passed by Shareholders in the extraordinary general meeting held on November 27, 2015, the name of the Company was changed to ‘Campus Activewear Private Limited. Subsequently company was converted into public company and name of the company was changed to ‘Campus Activewear Limited’, and a fresh certificate of incorporation dated November 22, 2021, was issued to Company by the RoC.

Business of the Company

  • Company is primarily engaged in the manufacturing, distribution and sales of sports and athleisure footwear products.
  • It offers multiple choices across styles, colour palettes, price points and an attractive product value proposition.
  • The company manufactures and distributes a variety of footwear like Running Shoes, Walking Shoes, Casual Shoes, Floaters, Slippers, Flip Flops and Sandals, available in multiple colours, styles.
  • It has sold more than 7.00 million pairs through online channels since Fiscal 2019 up to December 31, 2021.
  • It is the largest sports and athleisure footwear brand in India, both in terms of value and volume in Fiscal 2021.
  • It has a pan-India trade distribution network.
  • It has over 425 distributors directly servicing and fulfilling orders of over 19,200 geographically mapped retailers at a pan-India level as on December 31, 2021.
  • The Company had an approximately 17% market share in the branded sports and athleisure footwear industry in India by value for Fiscal 2021.
