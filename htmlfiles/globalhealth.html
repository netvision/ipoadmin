Incorporation

The company was incorporated as ‘Global Health Private Limited’ on August 13, 2004, in New Delhi, India as a private limited company under the Companies Act, 1956. Subsequently, Company was converted into a public limited company under the Companies Act, 2013, and consequently, the name of the Company was changed to ‘Global Health Limited’, and a fresh certificate of incorporation dated August 11, 2021, was issued by the RoC.

History

  • 2009:
    • Establishment and commencement of operations of Medanta – the Medicity Hospital at Gurugram.
  • 2011:
    • 1 st National Accreditation Board for Hospitals and Healthcare Providers (“NABH”) accreditation and for Testing and Calibration Laboratories accreditation for the hospital at Gurugram.
  • 2013:
    • Anant Investments (i.e., Investor Selling Shareholder) invested in the Company.
    • 1st Joint Commission International (“JCI”) accreditation for the hospital at Gurugram.
  • 2015:
    • Dunearn Investments (Mauritius) Pte Ltd invested in the Company.
    • GHPPL entered into a 33-year concession agreement with Governor of Bihar for the development, operation and maintenance of a proposed hospital on public-private partnership at Patna.
  • 2019:
    • Commencement of operations of new hospital at Lucknow by MHPL.
  • 2020:
    • Gurugram hospital outpatient department pharmacy taken in-house.
    • Launch of outpatient department at hospital at Patna by GHPPL.
    • Hospital at Gurugram ranked as the best private hospital in India by Newsweek.
  • 2021:
    • Lucknow hospital, Indore hospital and Ranchi hospital outpatient department pharmacy taken in-house.
    • Launch of outpatient department pharmacy at south Delhi clinic.
    • Hospital at Gurugram Awarded as ‘Best Multi-Speciality Hospital – National’ at ‘The Economic Times Healthcare Awards, 2021.’
  • 2022:
    • Patna hospital’s inpatient department facility commenced operations.
    • Launch of Patna hospital outpatient department pharmacy.
    • Hospital at Gurugram featured in the top 250 global hospitals by Newsweek.
    • Hospital at Gurugram ranked as the best multi-specialty private hospital in North India and the second-best private hospital in India as per Best All India Multi Speciality Hospital Ranking 2022 by Outlook and NEB Research.

Holding and Subsidiary

  • Company does not have any holding company, joint ventures, or associates.
  • Company has three subsidiaries namely Global Health Patliputra Private Limited, Medanta Holdings Private Limited, and GHL Pharma & Diagnostic Private Limited.

Business of the Company

  • It is one of the largest private multi-speciality tertiary care providers operating in the North and East regions of India.
  • The company has key specialities in cardiology and cardiac science, neurosciences, oncology, digestive and hepatobiliary sciences, orthopaedics, liver transplant, and kidney and urology.
  • Company has a network of five hospitals currently in operation at Gurugram, Indore, Ranchi, Lucknow and Patna under the brand “Medanta”.
  • It also has one hospital in Noida, which is under construction.
  • The Company provides healthcare services in over 30 medical specialties and engages over 1,300 doctors led by experienced department heads, spanning an area of 4.7 million sq. ft., operational hospitals have 2,467 installed beds As at June 30, 2022.
