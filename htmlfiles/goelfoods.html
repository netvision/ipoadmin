Incorporation

Company was originally incorporated on January 31, 1996 as a Private Limited Company as “Goel Food Products Private Limited” with the Registrar of Companies, West Bengal. Subsequently, pursuant to a special resolution of Shareholders passed in the Extraordinary General Meeting held on February 17, 2022, Company was converted from a Private Limited Company to Public Limited Company and consequently, the name of the Company was changed to ‘Goel Food Products Limited’.

History

Holding and Subsidiary:

  • Company does not have any subsidiary or Joint Venture Company. It has one holding company, namely, Hilltop Healthcare Centre Limited

Business of the Company

  • It started as an Indian Snacks and Sweets Manufacturing Company.
  • Currently It have 8 banquets halls and 2 Indian sweets and snack shop and one hotel and one guest house.
  • It provides services such as organizing events like Marriages, Sangeet, Ring Ceremony, Birthday Parties, Anniversary Parties, Kitty Parties, Corporate Events, Kirtans (Devotional Singing), Thread Ceremony at banquets hall and serve Indian snack and Sweets at retail shop.
  • Company also generates rent income from hotels and guest house.
  • Company run its business under the name “BIKA” at various locations in Kolkata.
