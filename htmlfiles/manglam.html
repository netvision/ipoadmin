Incorporation

Company was originally incorporated on December 11, 1995 as ‘Temchem Exports Private Limited’, as a private limited company, under the provisions of the Companies Act, 1956. Thereafter, the name of the Company was changed to ‘Hindprakash Exim Private Limited’ on April 30, 2007. Later on, the name of the company was changed to ‘Mangalam Worldwide Private Limited’ on October 13, 2014. Subsequently, company was converted into a public limited company and the name of the company was changed to “Mangalam Worldwide Limited” and fresh certificate of incorporation dated March 16, 2022 was issued by the Registrar of Companies, Ahmedabad.

Holdings and Subsidiaries:

  • The Company have one subsidiary, namely, Mangalam Saarloh Private Limited. It does not have any holding or joint venture company.

Business of the Company

  • Company is mainly engaged in manufacturing of Stainless Steel (SS) Billets and rolling of SS Flat Bars.
  • It is also engaged in trading of steel - scrap, ferro alloys, etc.
  • It manufactures SS billets of 200 series, 300 series (including series 304 and series 316), 400 series - grades in Austenitic, Ferritic, Martensitic and special chemical composition grades steel like 17/4 PH, Duplex and Super duplex Steel.
  • Its products are used across industries like food and dairy equipments, Utensils, oil and gas, aerospace and medical devices among others.
  • Its major customers are spread across states - Rajasthan, Gujarat, Maharashtra and Uttarpradesh.
  • It has also started exporting products to Brazil.
  • It also does the manufacturing of its products on job work basis from third party manufacturers.
  • Its fully integrated infrastructure consists of steel melting shops, rolling mills and finishing machines.

Manufacturing Facilities

Unit - I

  • It is located at Halol, Gujarat.
  • It is taken along with plant and machinery on lease & license basis from the lessors M/s. Agarwal Mittal Concast Private Limited.
  • It has installed manufacturing capacity of 66000 MT/p.a. of Stainless Steel (SS) Billets.

Unit - II

  • It is located at Changodar, Ahmedabad, Gujarat.
  • t is taken along with plant and machinery on lease & license basis from the lessors M/s. Vicor Stainless Private Limited.
  • It has installed rolling capacity of 90,000 MT/p.a. of SS Flat bars.
