Incorporation

The company was incorporated as Technopack Polymers Pvt. Ltd. under the provisions of the Companies Act, 2013. The company was subsequently converted into a public limited company pursuant to a special resolution passed by the Shareholders on 11th July 2022 and the name of the Company was changed to “Technopack Polymers Limited.” A fresh certificate of incorporation was issued by the RoC on 15th July 2022.

Holding and Subsidiary

  • Company does not have any holdings, subsidiaries, and Joint ventures.

Business of the Company

  • Technopack is a leading manufacturer, and supplier of plastic and paper products for FMCG packaging.
  • The company manufactures high-quality PET preform on 48 cavity state-of-art, world-class Milacron Machine, and ACME Mould.
  • It also manufactures high-quality CCM CAP.
  • The Company has fully automatic machines for the manufacture of PET Preform & Cap Closure at Rajkot, Gujarat.

Major Product Ranges offered by the Company:

  • PET Preforms
    • It is used in synthetic fibers; beverage, food, and other liquid containers; thermoforming applications; and engineering resins often in combination with glass fiber.
    • These Pet Preforms can be availed in any colour and size, as per the special requirements of the clients.
    • The Company manufactures 3 types of PET preform for the packaged drinking water industry:
      1. Alaska 28 mm 3 start 8.20 gram-250ml
      2. Alaska 28 mm 3 start 12.70 gram-500ml
      3. Alaska 28 mm 3 start 17.80 gram-1000ml
  • HDPE Cap Closure
    • Cap-Closure is made of HDPE plastic granules.
    • Alaska 27 mm 3 start – 1.35-gram cap closure suitable for 28 mm Alaska pet performs in many different colours.
