Incorporation

Company was started in 2014 by Mr. Sayyed Hamid with headquarters in Cochin, Kerala. Company was originally incorporated as “Fone4 Communications (India) Private Limited” on May 8, 2014. Subsequently, the company was converted into Public Limited Company vide a fresh certificate of incorporation issued by ROC Ernakulam, consequently name of the company was changed to “Fone4 Communications (India) Limited”.

Business of the Company

  • Company has a wide range of offerings through both online as well as offline platform for electronic gadgets and accessories for Smart Phones, Laptops, Smart Television etc.
  • Its major manufactures include Apple, Samsung, Oppo, Vivo, Xiaomi, Nokia, Redmi, Techno, Karbon, One Plus, Dell, Lenovo, Asus, HP, Acer, Avita, TCL, LG, Sansui etc.
  • Company has 25 Showrooms with the brand name 'FONE4' in all major cities of Kerala including Calicut, Cochin, Kollam, Kottayam, Malappuram, Palakkad, Thrissur, Trivandrum.
  • Company has emerged as one of the most trusted online platform outfits for purchase of Electronics gadgets and accessories in the State of Kerala.
  • The bestselling products and brands of the company are Samsung, Vivo, Oppo, Xiaomi which contribute approximately 79% of the company's revenue
