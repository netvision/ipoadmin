Incorporation

Company was originally incorporated on September 26, 2019 as a private limited company as “Sailani Tours N Travels Private Limited” under the Companies Act, 2013 with the Registrar of Companies, Central Registration Centre. Subsequently, company was converted into a public limited company and the name of the company was changed to “Sailani Tours N Travels Limited” and a fresh certificate of incorporation dated October 07, 2021 was issued by Registrar of Companies, Kolkata, West Bengal.

History

  • 2019
    • Opening up franchises in various states in India.
  • 2021
    • Conversion into Public Limited as “Sailani Tours N Travels Limited”

Holdings and Subsidiaries:

  • Company does not have any holding, subsidiary and joint venture.

Business of the Company

  • Company is a tour & travels operator and belongs to the Tourism and Hospitality sector.
  • It caters to the overall travel needs of Indian and International travellers in the state of Kolkata.
  • Its business can be broadly categorized as Leisure Travel, Corporate Travel, Forex and Visa Processing.
  • It also provides travel related foreign exchange & payment solutions.
  • It’s all activities are centered around pax satisfaction in terms of budget, destination, pre and post sales dealing, experience during the course of journey.
  • Its end to end travel solutions includes land, air and cruise bookings, hotel bookings, in-transit arrangements, local sightseeing, visa, passport and medical insurance assistance and such other destination management services.
  • Company hires tour operators who act as a guide and operates tour for the customers
  • Company also provides online services through website www.sailanitours.com.

Principles of the Company

Company works on two principles:

  1. Passenger availing the services should not be dissatisfied on any count.
  2. All transactions are to be honest and transparent to all stakeholders.

Services

  • Leisure Travel:
    1. Domestic Travel: It includes holiday packages for travel in India.
    2. Outbound Travel: It includes holiday packages for Indian Nationals travelling out of India.
  • Corporate travel:
    1. In corporate travel, company offers a full range of customized business travel services.
    2. Company makes travel arrangements for corporate clients to cater to their business meetings, conferences, events, etc.
    3. It also offers corporate travel as an incentive for their employees and business partners.
