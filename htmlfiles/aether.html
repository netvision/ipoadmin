Business of the Company

  • It is a speciality chemical manufacturer in India focused on producing advanced intermediates and speciality chemicals involving complex and differentiated chemistry and technology core competencies.
  • It is the sole manufacturer in India of 4MEP, MMBC, T2E, OTBN, NODG, DVL and Bifenthrin Alcohol.
  • Its product portfolio comprised over 25 products As of March 31, 2022.
  • Its customers include more than 160 multinational, global, regional and local companies.
  • Company exports its products to more than 18 countries including Italy, Spain, Germany, the United States and other parts of the world.
  • It had a specialized R&D team of 164 scientists and engineers.
  • It has one of the largest pilot plants in the world with 106 reactors installed, for both batch as well as continuous reaction technology.
  • It has two sites at Sachin in Surat (Gujarat, India), Manufacturing Facility 1 is an approximately 3,500 square meters facility and Manufacturing Facility 2 spans approximately 10,500 square meters and acts as a large-scale manufacturing facility with an installed capacity of 6,096 MT per annum.

Products and Services

Its business is based on three business models:

  1. Large scale manufacturing of own speciality chemicals and intermediates.
  2. CRAMS (contract research and manufacturing services)
  3. Contract manufacturing/exclusive manufacturing.
