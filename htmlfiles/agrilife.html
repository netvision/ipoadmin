Incorporation

Company was originally incorporated as a Private Limited Company ‘Tiff Care Industries Private Limited’ on November 08, 2019 under the Companies Act, 2013 under the Registrar of Companies – Maharashtra. Thereafter, the name of the company was changed to “Healthy Life Agritec Private Limited” on April 22, 2020. Subsequently, company was converted into a public limited company and the name of the Company was changed to “Healthy Life Agritec Limited” and a fresh certificate of incorporation dated March 08, 2022 was issued by the RoC, Maharashtra.

History

  • 2020 :
    • Company started its operation as trading of Milk and Live Chicken.
  • 2021 :
    • M/s. Cronosglobal Investments & Holdings Private Limited became holding company.
  • 2022 :
    • Acquired 2 subsidiary companies namely, Healthy Life Agro Private Limited and Healthy Life farm Private Limited.
    • Company got shareholders’ approval to raise funds through Initial Public Offer (IPO) and get listed.

Holdings and Subsidiaries:

  • Holding Company: Holding company is Cronosglobal Investments & Holdings Private Limited.
  • Subsidiary Companies: The Company has two Subsidiary COmpanies, namely, Healthy Life Agro Private Limited and Healthy Life Farms Private Limited.

Business of the Company

  • Company is engaged in the business of trading of Raw Milk, Live Chicken and Agro products.
  • It supplies raw milk in the state of Maharashtra and live chicken and agro products in the state of Maharashtra and Karnataka.
  • It procures raw cow milk from milk farmers in Maharashtra.
  • It procures poultry from poultry growers in Maharashtra and Karnataka.
  • It has also engaged procurement partners, to co-ordinate the milk procurement process with milk farmers and supply raw milk to milk wholesalers.
  • During 2021-22, company has sold 39.20 Lakh litres of raw cow milk and 7.69 Lakh Kgs of live chicken.
