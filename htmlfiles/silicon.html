Incorporation

Company was incorporated as ‘Silicon Rental Solutions Private Limited’ under the Companies Act, 2013, pursuant to a certificate of incorporation dated January 28, 2016. Subsequently company was converted into public company and name of the company was changed to “Silicon Rental Solutions Limited.”

History

  • 2016-17: Crossed Revenue of Rs. 5 crores
  • 2021-22: Crossed Revenue of Rs. 20 crores

Holding and Subsidiary

  • Company does not have any holding, subsidiary, associate, and joint venture company.

Business of the Company

  • Company is an IT equipment outsourcing company.
  • It is engaged in providing end to end IT equipments on a rental and returnable basis in India.
  • It provides laptops, desktops, printers, servers and other peripherals like CCTV cameras, projectors, storage devices etc. on rental basis mainly to small, medium, and large corporate.
  • It also sells the used IT equipments to clients as per their requirements.
  • It provides IT Products for short period of time ranging from 1 day to longer period upto 36 months based on the customer requirements.
  • It provides products of all major brands like HP, Dell, Lenovo, Apple, Asus, Intel, AMD, Gigabyte, Sonicwall, Dlink, Cisco etc.
  • Its presence in almost 19 states and Union territory in India.

Property Details:

  • Company has taken its Registered office and its three branch offices located at Pune, New Delhi, and Kolkata on lease.
