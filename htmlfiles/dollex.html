Incorporation

The company was incorporated as a private limited company namely “Dollex Agrotech Private Limited” under the Companies Act, vide certificate of incorporation dated June 10, 2013, Subsequently, the company was converted into a Public Limited Company in pursuance of a special resolution passed by the members of the Company & name of the company changed to ‘Dollex Agrotech Limited.’

History

  • 2017 :
    • Alteration of the object clause of the Memorandum of Association by adding the business of manufacturing sugar and its by-products. The company set up a new sugar manufacturing plant in Datia.
  • 2018:
    • The promoter decided to set up a sugar manufacturing plant, in the name of the company ‘Dollex Industries Limited’ which was later merged with an unlisted company, ‘Parvati Sweeteners and Power Ltd’ and the promoters sold their shareholding in that Company & currently has no control neither by shareholding nor by any managerial rights.
  • 2022:
    • Alteration of the object clause of the Memorandum of Association by adding manufacturing and processing of grains and by-products such as fuel, bio-fuels, ethanol, and all its by-products.

Holdings and Subsidiaries:

  • The company does not have any Holdings, Subsidiaries or associates, or joint ventures.

Business of the Company

  • The company is engaged in the business of manufacturing & trading of sugar with captive power co-generation capabilities.
  • It has received in-principal approval from the Department of Food & Public Distribution from the Government of India for establishing distilleries of 200 KLPD capacity.
  • In addition to sugar, it also produces and sells its by-products such as molasses, press mud, and bagasse.
  • The bagasse is used to generate power. Further, the company is into captive power generation with a capacity of 3 MW for its own use and not in commercial power generation and distribution as of date. The power is generated from bagasse to run the plant at Datia.
  • It is planning to step into the business of Ethanol production under the EBP Program by the Ministry of Petroleum and Natural Gas.
  • The Company manufactures & trades sugar in wholesale & sells it in a package of 50kgs to anyone who wants to buy it, therefore it has no distributor as of date & it sells sugar to clients directly without involving any distributor.
  • The company has been allotted an area of 7600 hectares by the government to take the sugarcane produce from the farmers.
