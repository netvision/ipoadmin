Incorporation

The Company was originally incorporated as a Private Limited Company under the Companies Act, 1956 in the name of “DAPS Advertising Private Limited”. Subsequently, the Company was converted into a public limited company and the name of the Company was changed to “DAPS Advertising Limited” vide a special resolution passed by the Shareholders at the Extra Ordinary General Meeting held on March 14, 2018. A fresh Certificate of Incorporation consequent upon conversion into a public limited company was granted to the Company on September 20, 2018, by the Registrar of Companies, Kanpur.

Business of the Company

  • The Company is engaged in the business of providing advertising agency services.
  • Its offering includes a range of advertising media services consisting of print media, electronic media, and outdoor media services which cover advertisement modes such as newspapers, brochures, magazines, television channels, FM channels, and display of outdoor hoardings, etc.
  • They are an INS (Indian Newspaper Society) accredited Company.
  • The Company acts as an intermediary between clients and the media and the scope of services includes assisting clients in choosing the optimal advertisement channel in accordance with their budget and regional preference, designing of advertisements (through an in-house team or third party), negotiating and booking ad space with chosen media agency and ensuring the publication of requisite advertisement.

Services

  • Print Media Print Media covers advertisements in a printed medium such as a newspaper, magazine, or trade journal.
  • Electronic Media FM is the primary electronic media source in which it operates and offers advertisement services.
  • Outdoor Media Signboards and hoardings are the primary outdoor advertising media source and also organize events and roadshows for the clients as part of Outdoor media services.
