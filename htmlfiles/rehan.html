Incorporation

Company was incorporated as Shivani Rolling Mills Private Limited on June 26, 1984 under the Companies Act, 1956. Subsequently name of the company was changed to Shree Ghantakarna Rolling Mills Private Limited. Further name of the company was changed to Rhetan Rolling Mills Private Limited. Further again name was changed to Rhetan TMT Private Limited vide Special resolution dated December 31, 2021.The fresh certificate of incorporation consequent of name change was issued on January 07, 2022 by the Registrar of Companies, Ahmedabad. The status of the Company was changed to public limited and the name of the Company was changed to Rhetan TMT Limited.

Histroy

  • 1986 :
    • Commenced production of TMT bars at Manufacturing facility located at 325, Karannagar, Kadi-Kalol Road, Taluka Kadi, Dist. Mehsana, Gujarat - 382715.
  • 1992 :
    • Uttar Gujrat Vij Company Limited (UGVCL) filed special civil suit No. 49 of 1992 for recovery of electricity charges incurred at the factory site.
  • 1994 :
    • Revaluation of Land was done by the Management of the Company.
  • 2001 :
    • Closure of Plant due to internal financial constrain as well as unfavourable Market conditions.
  • 2002:
    • Electricity connection was discontinued at the factory site due to non-payment of dues.
  • 2012:
    • State of Gujarat vide notification dated March 29, 2012 had declared Amnesty Scheme for settlement of outstanding claims of consumers.
  • 2017:
    • Company applied to the UGVCL/ Industries ‘Commissioner to re-consider it for eligibility under the Scheme.
  • 2018:
    • Company availed low-tension (LT) load of 13.33 KVA from Uttar Gujarat Vij Company Ltd (UGVCL) for carrying out modernization and overhauling of existing Plant and Machinery and for installation of new machinery at the factory site.
    • Company availed high-tension (HT) load of 1500 KVA from Uttar Gujarat Vij Company Ltd (UGVCL) for Manufacturing.
  • 2019:
    • Company re-commenced commercial production of TMT bars.
  • 2021:
    • Sub-Division of Equity shares from 100 to 10 each vide special resolution passed at general meeting dated August 29, 2021.
  • 2022:
    • Company crossed turnover of 50 crores.

Holdings and Subsidiaries:

  • The Company has one holding company, Ashoka Metcast Limited. It doesnt have any subsidiary or joint venture company.

Properties:

  • Company owned one property which is used as manufacturing unit, located at 325, Karannagar, Kadi-Kalol Road, Taluka Kadi, Dist. Mehsana, Gujarat.

Business of the Company (As of December 31, 2022)

  • Company manufactures TMT Bars and Round Bars which are primarily used in construction industry.
  • Company is involved in manufacturing and supplying of a vast portfolio of products and services of steel.
  • Its products have been used in construction of dams, bridges, residential and commercial towers and major infrastructure projects in Gujarat.
  • Company owns and operates a Steel Rolling Mill with an aggregate installed capacity of 30,000 MTPA.
  • Company is an IS 1786: 2008 certified company.
  • The company's plant is located in Gujarat and is spread over 15,000 sq. yards.
  • The plant is fully geared with latest technologies.
