Incorporation

Company was originally incorporated on July 7, 2003 as a private limited company as “Shri Raj Manufacturing and Projects Private Limited” under the Companies Act, 1956 with the Registrar of Companies, Madhya Pradesh & Chhattisgarh. Thereafter, the name of the company was changed to “Jayant Infratech Private Limited” on January 01, 2008. Subsequently, company was converted into a public limited company and the name of the company was changed to “Jayant Infratech Limited” and a fresh certificate of Incorporation dated March 29, 2022 was issued by the RoC, Chhattisgarh.

Business of the Company

  • Company is engaged in the field of railway infrastructure development right from design to energization.
  • Its major work encompasses electrification of new & existing railway tracks.
  • Its operations involve design, drawing, supply, erection and commissioning of 25KV, 50Hz single phase traction overhead equipment.
  • It has erected several traction substations in a span of 20 years.
  • Its key clients include various zones of the Indian Railways like Eastern Railway, Northern Railway, East Coast Railway, North Frontier Railway, East Central Railway, Central Organisation for Railway Electrification (CORE), etc.
  • Company is based out in Chhattisgarh and also offers services to coal mines.
  • It has over the years developed many siding (to load and transport coals) in the state to facilitate coal mines.
