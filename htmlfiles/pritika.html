Incorporation

The Company was incorporated as ‘Pritika Engineering Components Private Limited’ on February 20, 2018, as a private limited company under the Companies Act, 2013 with the Registrar of Companies, Chandigarh. Subsequently, Company was converted to a public limited company and consequently, the name of the Company was changed to ‘Pritika Engineering Components Limited,’ and a fresh certificate of incorporation dated April 21, 2022, was issued to Company by the Registrar of Companies, Punjab & Chandigarh at Chandigarh.

Business of the Company

  • Company is engaged in the business of manufacturing precision machined components primarily for the automotive industry, especially for tractors, trucks, and other commercial vehicles, etc.
  • The Company caters to the tractor industry in the automotive sector with major dependency on Original Equipment Manufacturers.
  • They supply products directly to OEM and through promoter company Pritika Auto Industries Limited and group company Pritika Industries Limited.
  • The Company manufactures various Tractors & Automobile components like End Covers, Cover Sealed Brakes, Differential Case, Cover Hydraulic Lift, Cover Transcase, Front Wheel hub, Fly Wheel Housing, Rear Axle Casings, Hydraulic Lift Covers, Brake Housing & Front Engine Supports, etc. with the Vision to be the First choice among Original Equipment Manufacturers for Machined Castings.
  • Its Mission is to Produce 35,000 MT of Machined Castings by the Year 2025.
  • In FY22, they manufactured 6619 MT with an installed capacity of 12000 MT.
  • In FY20, FY21, FY22 & H1FY23 unutilized capacity was 59%, 56%, 45% & 16% respectively, which shows a huge potential for growth in business

Financial Riots

  • The Net Profit Margin was 4.16% for the half year ended September 30, 2022.
  • The EBITDA Margin for FY21 & FY20 remained consistently healthy at 10.88% & 12.09% respectively and the total revenue from FY20 to FY22 grew by 62.54% CAGR.
  • The Long-term Debt to Equity ratio has consistently decreased from 3.08 in FY20 & 1.32 in FY21, to 1.02 in FY22. It has remained at 1.02 in FY23 as well.
