Incorporation

The company was incorporated as “Nashik Vintners Limited” as a public limited company under the Companies Act, 1956, pursuant to a certificate of incorporation issued by the RoC on February 26, 2003. Subsequently, the name of the Company was changed to “Nashik Vintners Private Limited”. And again, the name of the company was changed to “Sula Vineyards Private Limited”. On the conversion of the Company to a public limited company, pursuant to a resolution passed by Shareholders on December 27, 2021, the name was changed to “Sula Vineyards Limited” and a fresh certificate of incorporation was issued by the RoC on February 11, 2022.

Business of the Company

  • It is India's largest wine producer and seller as of March 31, 2022.
  • It distributes wines under a bouquet of popular brands.
  • It produces 56 different labels of wine at four owned and two leased production facilities located in the Indian states of Maharashtra and Karnataka.
  • Its wines are classified under four broad categories, namely the 'Elite' category with 21 labels, followed by the 'Premium' category with 13 labels, the 'Economy' category with 13 labels, and the 'Popular' category offering 9 labels.
  • It also imports and distributes 21 international labels (18 wine labels, two vodka labels, and one brandy label)
  • Sula Vineyards serviced close to 8,000 hotels, restaurants, and caterers, which makes the company the leader in terms of footprint among wine players in India.

Businesses can be broadly classified into two categories:

  1. The production of wine, the import of wines and spirits, and the distribution of wines and spirits (the “Wine Business”)
  2. The sale of services from ownership and operation of wine tourism venues, including vineyard resorts and tasting rooms.
