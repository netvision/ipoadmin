Incorporation

Company was originally incorporated as “Agni Power and Electronics Private Limited” on August 25, 1995 under the Companies Act, 1956 under the Registrar of Companies, Kolkata, West Bengal. Thereafter, the name of the company was changed to “Agni Green Power Private Limited” on March 04, 2022. Subsequently, company was converted into a public limited company and the name of the company was changed to “Agni Green Power Limited” and a fresh certificate of incorporation dated April 01, 2022 was issued by ROC, Kolkata.

Business of the Company

  • Company mainly operates in the following segments:
    • Engineering, procurement and construction (“EPC”),
    • Installation, commissioning, operation and maintenance of Solar Photovoltaic Power Plants and Bio-Gas Plants.
    • Manufacturing, assembling of solar lighting system and other solar products.
  • Its product and services portfolio ranges from home/ street lighting systems to megawatt level power plants.
  • It also provides consultancy services from design to implementation, of Solar PV projects.
  • Its quality ethos constitutes quality consciousness and frictionless cooperation.
  • Its key clients ranging from Govt. bodies to PSUs and Private enterprises.
  • It majorly undertakes govt. or semi-govt. projects by participating in tenders/bids.
  • Its total strength of manpower is 75 employees including directors.
  • Its workforce comprises of skilled technicians and specialized installers.
  • It has a dedicated R&D facility to research and develop new indigenous products.
  • It also has four branch offices in Chhattisgarh, Assam, Mizoram and Tripura.

Success Mantra

  • Precise planning, meticulous selection of premium components and on-time delivery/ completion of projects.
