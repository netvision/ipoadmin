Incorporation

The company was originally incorporated as “Rite Zone Chemicals India Private Limited” on March 10, 2015, as a private limited company. Subsequently, its name was changed to “Rite Zone Chemcon India Private Limited”. The company was converted into a Public Limited Company and the name of the Company was changed to “Rite Zone Chemcon India Limited.” A Fresh Certificate of Incorporation consequent upon conversion to a public limited company was issued by the Registrar of Companies, Mumbai, Maharashtra on June 02, 2022.

History

  • 2019
    • Annual revenues from operations crossed ₹ 5 Crores.
  • 2021
    • Receipt of ISO 9001:2015 certification in respect of manufacturing and marketing of construction chemicals.
  • 2022
    • Takeover of the business of Bhavesh Babulal Bhandari (HUF) in the name and style of Maruti Infrachem Solutions.

Holding and Subsidiary

  • Company does not have any holding, subsidiary, associate, and joint venture company.

Business of the Company

  • Company deals in a range of construction chemicals and concrete products.
  • Its range of products include Concrete Admixture, Water Proofing Systems, Concrete Lubricator, Engineering Grouts, Industrial Flooring, Concrete Repairs, Curing Compound & Mould Release Agent, Surface Treatment, Tiling & Block Products.
  • It offers a complete construction solution by supplying a complete range of various chemical products related to civil construction, infrastructure, building & structure including pre-construction or post construction as well as supplying all types of concrete required for construction and infrastructure developments.
  • Company outsources the production of its chemical products to a third-party manufacturer.
  • Also, they have tied up with concrete manufacturers in Maharashtra for the production of concrete.

Geographical Presence
