Incorporation

The company was incorporated as ‘Dharni Online Services Private Limited’ on November 12, 2015, as a private limited company under the Companies Act, 2013. Further, the name of the Company was changed to “Dharni Capital Services Private Limited.” Subsequently, the Company was converted to a public limited company and consequently, the name of the Company was changed to ‘Dharni Capital Services Limited’, and a fresh certificate of incorporation dated May 17, 2022, was issued to the Company by the Registrar of Companies Bangalore, Karnataka.

Histroy

Business of the Company

  • Services provided by the company include Mutual Funds, Real Estate, Home Loans, Financial Advisory Services, Equity Investment, Accounting, and Assurance Services.
  • It offers a technology-enabled, comprehensive investment and financial services platform with end-to-end solutions critical for financial products distribution and presence across both online and offline channels.
