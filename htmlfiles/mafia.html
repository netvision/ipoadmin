Incorporation

The company was incorporated as ‘Mafia Trends Private Limited’ on February 07, 2017, as a private limited company under the Companies Act, 2013 with the Registrar of Companies, Gujarat, Ahmedabad. The company was converted from a private limited company to a public limited company and consequently, the name of the company was changed to ‘Mafia Trends Limited,’ and a fresh certificate of incorporation dated January 12, 2022, was issued to the Company by the Registrar of Companies, Gujarat at Ahmedabad.

Holdings and Subsidiaries:

  • The company does not have any Holdings, Subsidiaries, Associates, or Joint Ventures.

Business of the Company

  • The business revolves around marketing and selling of Men’s & kid’s fashion such as Jeans, T-shirts, Shirts, Chinos, Formal trousers, and Formal shirts through a chain of clothing stores under the brand name “Mafia.”
  • It follows the concept of ‘Value Retail.’ It believes its locational presence would resonate with the customers untapped by competitors, and give them a pocket-friendly & more personalized shopping experience without comprising the quality, and brand value of their purchase.
  • The main office and legacy stores are based in Ahmedabad. In 2011, it started its operations. The first store was named ‘Mafia Western Outfit’ in Ahmedabad, Gujarat, and currently, it operates 8 stores spread across 3 cities and one warehouse in Gujarat.
  • The company adopts an integrated ‘Proactive’ and ‘Reactive’ approach to manage the supply chain in which it plans the supply schedule based on the forecasted demand. Separate ‘Above the Line’ (ATL) and ‘Below the Line’ (BTL) activities are conducted simultaneously to create a pull towards the brand and store.
  • It sources its products directly from the regions where such products are widely available or manufactured, to minimize procurement costs and offer quality products at such costs. The strong sourcing capability is backed by an efficient logistics network, which is supported by strong IT/ERP infrastructure systems.

Presence:

  • They are strategically placed across various small Indian towns and cities including Kalol, Vadodara, along with Ahmedabad.
  • Outside of Ahmedabad, it has strategically placed in tier-2 & tier-3 cities, as it aims to penetrate the target market of young students, a population belonging to transitioning class and middle-income groups who aspire to wear affordable fashionable outfits.
