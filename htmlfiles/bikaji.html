Incorporation

The company was originally incorporated as “Shivdeep Industries Limited” as a public limited company under the Companies Act, 1956 at Bikaner, pursuant to a certificate of incorporation dated October 6, 1995, issued by the RoC. Subsequently, the name of the company was changed to “Bikaji Foods International Limited”, to reflect the name of the brand in the name of the Company.

Holding and Subsidiary

  • Company does not have a holding, Associate, and Joint Venture company.
  • Company has three subsidiaries namely Petunt Food Processors Private Limited (“PFPPL”), Vindhyawasini Sales Private Limited (“VSPL”), Bikaji Maa Vindhyawasini Sales Private Limited (“BMVSPL”).
  • Company had a subsidiary, Bikaji Foods (London) Limited, which was dissolved on July 20, 2021.

Business of the Company

  • Company is engaged in selling Indian snacks and sweets.
  • It is the third largest ethnic snacks company in India with an international footprint.
  • It is the largest manufacturer of Bikaneri bhujia with an annual production of 29,380 tonnes.
  • It is the second largest manufacturer of handmade papad with an annual production capacity of 9,000 tonnes.
  • It is also the third largest player in the organized sweets market.
  • Its product range includes six principal categories: bhujia, namkeen, packaged sweets, papad, western snacks as well as other snacks which primarily include gift packs (assortment), frozen food, mathri range, and cookies.
  • In the three months ending June 30, 2022, it sold more than 300 products under the Bikaji brand.
  • It has over the years established market leadership in the ethnic snacks market in core states of Rajasthan, Assam, and Bihar with extensive reach, and has gradually expanded its footprint across India, with operations across 23 states and four union territories.
  • It has exported its products to 21 international countries, including countries in North America, Europe, the Middle East, Africa, and Asia Pacific.

Brand Ambassador

Amitabh Bachchan is appointed as the brand ambassador of Bikaji in the year 2019.

Manufacturing Facilities:

Company has seven operational manufacturing facilities with four facilities located in Bikaner (Rajasthan), one in Guwahati (Assam), one facility in Tumakuru (Tumkur) (Karnataka) held through of one the subsidiary Petunt Food Processors Private Limited to cater to the southern markets in India and one facility in Muzaffarpur (Bihar) held through the other subsidiary Vindhyawasini Sales Private Limited to cater to the core market of Bihar, respectively.

Properties:

  • It has own 51 properties which primarily include property used for an effluent treatment plant, a rooftop solar plant, and housing employees and staff.
  • It also took 26 properties on a leased and licensed basis which includes manufacturing facilities, godowns, and properties used by staff.
