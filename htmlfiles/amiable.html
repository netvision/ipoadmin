Incorporation

The Company was incorporated as “HSAM Clearing Services Private Limited” on December 02, 2002. Subsequently, the name of the company was changed to “Ami Clearing & Forwarding Private Limited” and then changed to “Amiable Logicstic (India) Private Limited.” Further, the name of the Company was corrected to “Amiable Logistic (India) Private Limited” by removing “c” from the word “Logicstic.” Again, the name of the Company was corrected to “Amiable Logistics (India) Private Limited” vide Special Resolution dated July 12, 2010, by adding “s” in the word “Logistic” and a fresh certificate of incorporation was issued on August 14, 2010, by the Registrar of Companies, Mumbai. The status of the Company was changed to public limited and the name of the Company was changed to “Amiable Logistics (India) Limited.”

Holding and Subsidiary

  • Company does not have any holding, subsidiary, associate, and joint venture company.

Business of the Company

  • The Company is in the business of providing services to importers and exporters for the smooth clearance of all kinds of imports and exports by air and sea as a clearing and forwarding agent and customs house broker.
  • The services also include import-export consultancy, international freight forwarding, recovery of all types of claims including customs & insurance, warehousing, and transportation through air, road, and sea.
  • They are members of “The Brihanmumbai Custom House Agent’s Association” popularly known as BCHAA or BCBA.
  • They are certified by Indian Customs in its Authorized Economic Operators (AEO) program.
  • They provide a complete range of services like Freight Forwarding (Sea freight & Air freight), Custom Clearance, Warehousing, Transportation, Multimodal Transportation, loading/unloading of items to facilitate the customers with end-to-end, solutions, and other related value-added services.
  • They cater services mainly in the domestic market specifically in the state of Maharashtra.
  • They strive to develop a long-term business relationship with customers by offering high-quality and value-added service while maintaining industry ethical standards, which are founded on the ability to help identify and recommend the best solution for each customer’s business environment.
