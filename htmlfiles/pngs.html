Incorporation

The company was originally incorporated as a Private Limited under the name “Resonant Consulting Private Limited” under the provisions of the Companies Act, 1956, and a Certificate of Incorporation was issued on March 25, 2009. Subsequently, the name of the Company was changed to “P. N. Gadgil & Sons Gargi Costume Jewellery Private Limited” and again, the name of the Company has changed to “PNGS Gargi Fashion Jewellery Private Limited.” Eventually, the status of the Company was changed to Public Limited and the name of the Company was changed to “PNGS Gargi Fashion Jewellery Limited.”

History

  • 2021
    • Change in control and ownership of the company to current promoters.
    • Setting up of brand “Gargi by P.N. Gadgil and Sons” under the fashion jewellery segment.
    • Commenced the operations under fashion jewellery segment.
  • 2022
    • Entered into an agreement with P.N. Gadgil & Sons Limited and P. N. Gadgil Art & Culture Foundation to have a point of sales (POS) counters at its existing 30 showrooms across the states of Maharashtra, Gujarat and Karnataka.

Holding and Subsidiary

  • The company does not have any Holdings, Subsidiaries or associates, or joint ventures.

Business of the Company

  • It is engaged in the retail business of costume and fashion jewellery under the brand name “Gargi by P. N. Gadgil & Sons” launched in 2021 under the artificial jewellery segment.
  • It deals in 92.5% certified sterling silver jewellery and brass jewellery, idols and other silverware and related gift items.
  • It offers a wide range of products from artificial jewellery segment for special occasions such as weddings and festivals to daily-wear jewellery for all ages, genders and across various price points.
  • Its portfolio offers to its customers a wide variety of traditional, Indo-western, & modern designs and jewellery.
  • It primarily sources finished jewellery from third-party manufacturing vendors located across India.
  • Products are mainly marketed using both online as well as offline marketing tools.
  • It also implemented quality control practices to sell hallmarked silver jewellery online through Amazon in line with the quality and purity metrics prescribed by BIS.

Products Portfolio:

  • Its product line includes earrings, necklaces, pendants, chains, finger rings, bracelets, anklets, nose pins, mangal sutra, pendant sets, bangles in brass or silver for females of age groups as well as male accessories such as rings, cufflinks, button set.
