Incorporation

Company was originally incorporated as PACE Sports and Entertainment Private Limited as Private Limited Company under the provisions of Companies Act, 2013 vide Certificate of Incorporation dated August 05,2015. Subsequently, the name of the Company was changed to Pace E-Commerce Ventures Private Limited. Thereafter Company was converted in to a public limited Company and consequently the name of the Company was changed to Pace E-Commerce Ventures Limited and a fresh certificate of incorporation was issued by the Registrar of Companies, Pune, dated August 08,2022.

History

There are no major events in the company since its incorporation.

Holding and Subsidiary

  • Company does not have any holding, subsidiary, associate, and joint venture company.

Business of the Company

  • Company started with imports and distribution of branded international sports apparels and extended to various branded merchandise and accessories.
  • Thereafter it acquired manufacturing and distribution licenses of some of the popular and biggest brands in the Kids Entertainment Industry.
  • It has its own e-commerce portal, www.cotandcandy.com offering a large range of products across different categories.
  • Later, portal expanded product categories to kids’ sports, kids’ fashion, kids’ furniture, and home textiles.
  • At present company operates across all cities in India through the online channels and most major cities through offline distribution of products.
  • Company focused on customization and personalisation across different product categories appealing to all age groups including kids, Teenagers and Young Adults.
  • The company also started catering to several requests for on-demand printing and manufacturing of products for both B2C and B2B Customers.
