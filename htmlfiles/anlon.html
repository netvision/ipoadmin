Incorporation

The company was originally incorporated as ‘Anlon Technology Solutions Private Limited’ on July 28, 2015, as a private limited company under the Companies Act, 2013 with the Registrar of Companies, Bangalore. Subsequently, the company was converted to a public limited company and consequently, the name of the Company was changed to ‘Anlon Technology Solutions Limited’, and a fresh certificate of incorporation dated June 13, 2022, was issued to the Company by the Registrar of Companies, Mumbai.

Business of the Company

  • The Company is primarily engaged in the business of providing engineering services for engineering systems, built on automotive chassis and allied areas, majorly for airports, high-rise buildings, and refineries.
  • The Company also sells spare parts for performing engineering services which is an ancillary part of the engineering services.
  • A spare parts hub based out of Bangalore and Mumbai enables quick availability of genuine spares, and accessories to customers for their critical needs.
  • In FY22, the company entered into a contract for the fabrication and installation of airport interior equipment.
  • Its Mobile Fire Fighting, Rescue, and Evacuation Equipment and allied services include Airport Rescue and Fire Fighting vehicles (ARFF), High rise building rescue, evacuation, and firefighting equipment, and Industrial Fire Engines.
  • Its Other Airport Services Includes Runway Rubber removal and paint removal machines, Runway sweeping and Bay cleaning, a Disabled Aircraft Recovery Kit (DARK), and a Supply of Airport interior components.

Business Segments

  1. Engineering services (Commissions from the Sale of Equipment, Spare Parts Trading, Maintenance, and Service.)
  2. Designing, Fabrication, and Installation Services.
