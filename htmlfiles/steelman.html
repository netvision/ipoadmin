Incorporation

The company was originally incorporated as a private limited company in the name of “Dwarka Prasad Bindal & Sons Hotels Private Limited” under the provisions of the Companies Act, 1956 with the Registrar of Companies, West Bengal. Later on, the name of the Company was changed to “Steelman Telecom Private Limited” and a fresh certificate of incorporation was issued by the Registrar of Companies, Kolkata, West Bengal. Subsequently, the Company was converted to a public limited company and the name of the Company was changed to “Steelman Telecom Limited” and a fresh certificate of incorporation consequent to conversion was issued on July 08, 2022, by the Registrar of Companies, Kolkata, West Bengal.

Business of the Company

  • It offers support services and solutions to address the network requirements of the Telecom industry. Over the years it has successfully implemented various projects and has been recognized by clients for the quality and timely execution of the projects undertaken.
  • It provides services for Network Survey and Planning, Installation and Commissioning, Network Testing and Optimization, Network Solutions, and Managed Services for network maintenance.
  • A strong technical background enables to effectively carry out end-to-end services thereby covering the entire network & technology service solutions.
  • It understands critical aspects of technology and utilizing the same to improve coverage, capacity & quality of today’s networks leads to network quality improvement; thereby enhancing every subscriber’s experience.
  • Quality is of paramount importance as it can essentially provide a competitive advantage.
