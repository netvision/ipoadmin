Incorporation

Company was incorporated on February 17, 2015 as Venus Pipes & Tubes Private Limited as a private limited company, under the Companies Act, 2013 and was granted a certificate of incorporation by the RoC. Subsequently company was converted into public company and name of the company was changed Venus Pipes & Tubes Limited. consequently, a fresh certificate of incorporation was issued by the RoC, on September 16, 2021.

History

  • 2015
    • Established manufacturing plant at Dhaneti, Bhuj-Bhachau highway, close to Mundar and Kandla Ports.
    • Commenced the production at manufacturing plant located at Dhaneti, Kutch, Gujarat.
  • 2016
    • Manufacturing facilities at Dhaneti have been accredited with management system standards certificate for compliance with ISO 9001: 2015, ISO 14001:2015 requirements.
  • 2017
    • Addition of stainless-steel seamless pipes to production line.
    • Started exporting stainless steel welded and stainless-steel seamless pipes to countries in the European Union, Brazil, UK, Israel etc.
    • Registered trademark under Class 6 of Trademarks Act.
    • Certified as quality-assurance system related to the material by The TÜV NORD Systems GmbH & Co. KG.
  • 2018
    • Manufacturing facilities at Dhaneti have been accredited with management system standards certificate for compliance with ISO 45001: 2018.
    • Company has received approval for manufacturing of Carbon Steel, Alloy Steel, Stainless Steel Welded & Seamless Pipes up to 400 mm NB / Tubes up to 101.60 mm O.D. & U-Tubes under inspection of Indian Boiler Regulations – 1950 from the Office of the Director of Boiler, Gujarat.

Holding and Subsidiary:

  • Company does not have a holding, subsidiary, associate or joint venture.

Business of the Company

  • It is one of the growing stainless-steel pipes and tubes manufacturer and exporter in India.
  • It’s one manufacturing plant is strategically located at Bhuj-Bhachau highway, Dhaneti (Kutch, Gujarat).
  • Manufacturing Facility has a total installed capacity of 10,800 MT per annum.
  • Its warehouse facility located at Ahmedabad.
  • Company sells its Products both in the domestic as well as the international markets.
  • Company exported products to 20 countries including Brazil, UK, Israel and countries in the European Union, etc.
  • It is Indian Boiler Regulations (IBR) certified manufacturer and supplier of stainless steel seamless and welded pipes/tubes.

Products of the Company

  • stainless steel high precision and heat exchange tubes.
  • stainless steel hydraulic and instrumentation tubes.
  • stainless steel seamless pipes.
  • stainless steel welded pipes.
  • stainless steel box pipes.

Product raw material through three main channels:

  • Domestic suppliers such as steel manufacturers, stockists and traders.
  • International suppliers from China, Indonesia, Malaysia and Singapore.
  • High sea purchases.
