Incorporation

The company was incorporated on May 06, 2011, as a private limited company under the provisions of the Companies Act, 1956 with the Registrar of Companies, Mumbai in the name and style of “Craft Financial Advisors Private Limited. Further, the name of the Company was changed to ‘Homesfy Realty Private Limited’ vide Fresh Certificate of Incorporation dated July 08, 2021. Subsequently, the Company was converted into a Public Limited company pursuant to which the name of the company was changed to “Homesfy Realty Limited” vide shareholder’s approval on October 17, 2022, and a fresh certificate of incorporation dated November 03, 2022.

Business of the Company

  • It is engaged in providing real estate broking services to Real Estate Developers, retail buyers/sellers, and investors for residential and commercial space.
  • It deals in newly constructed properties, whereby it assists the Real Estate Developers in selling and potential customers in purchasing the properties.
  • It operates business through direct selling from the in-house sales team and Direct Selling Agent listed on the mymagnet platform for referral services.
  • It runs its brokerage business in name of Homesfy and is well-partnered with the mymagnet platform.
  • Currently it generates 80.00% of revenue via direct selling and the remaining 20.00% via the mymagnet platform.
  • It currently maintains a network of 7 offices located in 4 cities, Mumbai, Pune, Noida, and Bangalore, which primarily engage in sales, marketing, and payment collection activities for the business.
  • It has housed an appropriate team of the talented pool, technology, customer-focused, and service-oriented teams to provide the customer with unmatched experience in the whole transaction process.

Business Model

  • Homesfy brokerage business is a combination of both offline and online presence.
  • It provides real estate broking-related services through its website www.homesfy.in.
  • Its operating model is backed by strong expertise in lead generation backed by process-driven digital marketing expertise and automation.
  • It also arranges property loans for the customers if required.
  • Developer Economics: It charges the Real Estate Developers commissions based on an agreed-upon rate. It generates an invoice and receives payments directly from the Real Estate Developers. Revenue generated on mymagnet platform is shared between Homesfy and the agent registered on mymagnet who has initiated the lead based on a predetermined sharing arrangement.
