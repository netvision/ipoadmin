Incorporation

Company was incorporated as a private limited company vide certificate of incorporation dated March 21, 2014. Subsequently pursuant to a special resolution passed at the EGM dated November 08, 2021 company was converted into a public limited company and consequently the name of the company was changed to ‘Empyrean Cashews Limited’ and a fresh certificate of incorporation dated November 22, 2021 was issued by the Registrar of Companies, Mumbai, Maharashtra.

History

  • 2014
    • Set up of processing unit.
  • 2016
    • Set up of packaging unit.
  • 2010
    • Ventured into manufacturing of Kitchen Equipments for Midday Meal Program & Other projects.
  • 2019
    • Company achieved a revenue of ₹ 50 Cr.
  • 2022
    • Acquisition of ‘Siddhivinayak Cashew Industries Private Limited.

Holding and Subsidiary:

  • Company does not have any holding and joint venture. It has one Subsidiary company, namely, Siddhivinayak Cashew Industries Private Limited.

Business of the Company

  • Company is engaged in processing raw cashew nuts into finished cashew kernels in different flavours.
  • It primarily caters to the domestic market with a network of more than thirty (30) distributors across India.
  • It procures majority of raw materials by way of import from certain African and Asian countries and also from the Konkan region of India.
  • It is also engaged in the business of organic manure which is prepared by using by-products like husk and other biodegradable material left after processing of the raw cashews.
  • Its product portfolio includes different flavours of cashews such as salted, pepper, chilli and assorted.
  • Products are supplied to different channels which include distributors, supermarkets, retailers as well as wholesalers.
  • Products of the Company are also sold over the counter to tourists and localities who visit packaging unit.
