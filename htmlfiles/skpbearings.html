Incorporation

Company was originally formed as a partnership firm under the name and style of “M/s. S K P Bearing Industries” pursuant to a deed of partnership dated November 21, 1991. Thereafter, it was converted into a private limited company “SKP Bearing Industries Private Limited” on January 06, 2022 under the Companies Act, 2013 and fresh a certificate of Incorporation dated January 06, 2022 was issued by RoC, Ahmedabad. Subsequently, company was converted into a public limited company “SKP Bearing Industries Limited” and a fresh Certificate of Incorporation dated February 15, 2022 issued by the RoC, Ahmedabad.

Holdings and Subsidiaries:

  • The Company does not have any holding, subsidiary or joint venture company.

Business of the Company (As of December 31, 2022)

  • Company is one of the manufacturers, exporter and supplier of finished rollers in India.
  • It manufacturers needle rollers, cylindrical rollers, pins and steel balls (“Rolling Elements”).
  • It supplies rolling elements to bearing manufactures and OEMs in domestic market in diverse industries such as textile, automobile, etc.
  • It also exports rolling elements to international customers in Brazil, Argentina, UAE, etc.
  • It supplies corrosion-free rollers which are designed by the experts and are made as per the specifications offered by the clients.
  • It has also installed solar plant and wind mills for generation and sale of power & electricity to various customers.
  • It has around 110 employees (including workers) at its plant and office.

Raw Materials:

  • The principal raw material used by the company is bearing steel wire rods and coil.
  • Company also uses raw materials like ENAD, Stainless Steel and others as specified by customers.
  • Its key suppliers of raw material are Jindal Steel &Power Ltd., Jayaswal Neco Industries Ltd, Etc.
  • It also imports raw material from France, China, South Korea, Japan, etc.

Manufacturing facility:

  • Company’s manufacturing facility unit is located at Surendranagar, Gujarat.
  • It is having a total area of approximately 16,160sq. mtrs.
  • This facility has machineries installed for manufacturing of rollers for 600 million pcs, and manufacturing of balls for 480 tons.

Solar plant and wind mills:

  • Company have only one solar plant installed at Agar, Madhya Pradesh.
  • It has three wind mills installed at Maharashtra, Gujarat and Madhya Pradesh.
